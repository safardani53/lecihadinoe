Lil Debbie (Rapper) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Jordan Mary Capozzi, known by her stage name Lil Debbie, is an American rapper, model and fashion designer. Debbie started her career in 2011 with the now defunct The White Girl Mob band, along with rappers Kreayshawn and V-Nasty. In 2012, Debbie began her solo career, releasing a series of singles with rapper Riff Raff and former White Girl band member Mob V-Nasty, some of which led to her first solo project, mixtape bearing Name Keep It Lit. In October 2013, Debbie released her first EP, titled Queen D. In March 2014, Debbie released her second EP, California Sweetheart, followed by California Sweetheart Pt. 2 later that same year. In November 2014, Debbie released her second mixtape, Young B! Tch. Debbie is also the DJ with the nickname "CAPOZZI".
