Who is Sam Elliot? 

Sam Elliott has made a name for himself as the go-to actor when a director decides to feature a cowboy role in any big screen project.

This is because he embodies everything he plays with his deep voice and mustache which makes him the authentic cowboy anyone would prefer.

Sam Elliot Bio

Sam Elliott is known in real life as Samuel Pack Elliott and was born on August 9th, 1944 in Sacramento, California, the United States of America.

He was born to his parents Glynn Mamie, a high school teacher, and Henry Nelson Elliott, an animal control specialist.

When he was just 13 years, Sam Elliott’s family moved to Oregon where he attended the David Douglas High School.

Eventually graduated in 1962 and later enrolled in the University of Oregon to study English and Psychology but after two terms he dropped out.

What disease does Sam Elliot have? 

There have been speculations that Sam Elliott is having a disease and in the minds of many people it might be true due to his age as of now but the truth is he doesn’t have any disease.

Sam Elliott doesn’t have any terminal disease however the speculations came up as a result of a role he played as Lee Hayden, an aging Western icon who was diagnosed with cancer in the 2017 movie titled The Hero.

Why did the rumor start? 

While becoming famous does, of course, have its perks, and boy, are there some major perks to be had, it does not come without its drawbacks as well.

One of the significant downsides to being a celebrity and constantly being in the public eye is that you will no doubt be the subject of rumors and downright lies.

Sometimes they’re simply harmless and unique, whereas other times, they’re downright hurtful.

Sam Elliott, who is one of the most recognized faces, and voices, in all of Hollywood, recently found out how rumors and misinformation on the internet can spread, when reports began emerging stating that he was unwell and was suffering from an undisclosed disease.

Needless to say, this will have been extremely distressing, not only for Sam but for his family as well.

With his fans rightfully concerned over the actor’s health following these reports, we thought we’d set the record and get to the bottom of things once and for all.

Sam Elliot Career 

Sam Elliot is a very well-known US actor who has been the recipient of numerous accolades over the course of his close to 6 decade-long career.

He has received a National Board of Review Award, along with being nominated for two Golden Globes, an Academy Award, Primetime Emmy Awards, and much more besides.

Sam, who is now close to 80 years of age, is well known for his deep, yet calming and masculine voice, his tall and slender physique, and of course, for his mustache.

Sam is perhaps best known for appearing in movies and TV shows from the Western genre.

He started his career in the late 1960s, appearing in Butch Cassidy and the Sundance Kid, as well as The Way West, Mission Impossible (the TV show), and Gunsmoke.
