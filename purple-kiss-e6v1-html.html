Purple Kiss (Band) - Members profile, Facts, Wiki, Achievements, Net worth 2024 (updated)

Purple Kiss is a South Korean girl group formed by RBW in 2020. The group consists of seven members: Park Ji-eun, Na Go-eun, Dosie, Ireh, Yuki, Chaein, and Swan. They are considered to be a self-producing group, with members involved in songwriting, choreography, and other aspects of their music and showmanship. The group released two pre-debut digital singles — "My Heart Skip a Beat" in November 2020, and "Can We Talk Again" in February 2021 — before their official debut on March 15, 2021, with the EP Into Violet.
