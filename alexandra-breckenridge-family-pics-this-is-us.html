See Actress Alexandra Breckenridges Family Hollywood Life

This Is Us has become one of NBC’s biggest hits over the years. And it is absolutely not shocking why it has either. Alexandra Breckenridge is just one of the many Hollywood hotties to grace the show’s set. Since 2016, she’s been playing Sophie, the on-again/off-again love interest of Justin Hartley’s character, Kevin Pearson.  

Her dramatic, tearjerker moments as Sophie naturally won her a Gold Derby Award in 2016 for being a part of the “Ensemble of the Year.” 

Prior to her massive success on This Is Us, the star had already established quite a name for herself in the industry, starring in Big Fat Liar (2002), She’s the Man (2006), FX’s American Horror Story: Murder House (2011), American Horror Story: Coven (2013), and AMC’s The Walking Dead (2016).
