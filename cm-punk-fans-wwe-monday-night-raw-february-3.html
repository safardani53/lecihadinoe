‘No Truth’ To Claims That CM Punk Fans Were Kicked Out

HollywoodLife.com reached out to our WWE sources, who deny that anyone was ejected from Monday Night Raw.

“The whole kicking out of fans is just trolling at its best,” the source explains. “No one was kicked out concerning anything surrounding CM Punk. The WWE expected the chants, but they didn’t kick anyone out, as that would have made it worse. There is no truth to it.”

CM Punk Thanks Fans For ‘All The Support’

Regardless of whether or not the fans were actually rejected for their pro-Punk agenda, the former WWE Superstar is clearly grateful for their unwavering support. Here’s the latest tweet sent out by CM Punk, a tribute to his fandom:

Thanks for all the support. Keep being you guys, it’s pretty cool.

— Coach (@CMPunk) January 27, 2014
