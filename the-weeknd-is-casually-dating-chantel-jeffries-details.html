The Weeknd Is Casually Dating Chantel Jeffries: Details

The Weeknd was definitely busy at the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival in Indio, California over the weekend. After performing his emotional set at the annual music festival Friday, April 13, the “Call Out My Name” singer was seen getting cozy with Chantel Jeffries a day later.

“Chantel and The Weeknd are definitely dating,” an insider exclusively reveals in the new issue of Us Weekly. “It’s new and nothing crazy serious.”

The close pal to the 24-year-old musician and model continues the new pair “dated years ago,” noting that it was even before Jeffries was linked to Justin Bieber in 2014, and before The Weeknd, 28, dated Selena Gomez, also Bieber’s ex. “They have been friends for years since,” the source continues. “They’re seeing if they can make it work.”

As Us previously reported, The Weeknd (real name Abel Tesfaye) was spotted kissing ex-girlfriend Bella Hadid just the day before the musician and Jeffries were seen cozying up together.

The runway model was quick to deny news of her getting close with her ex after E! News posted an Instagram of their reunion Monday, April 17. “It wasn’t me,” Hadid, 21, commented. However, multiple sources confirmed to Us that Hadid and The Weeknd were indeed canoodling at Coachella.
