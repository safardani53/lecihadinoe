KENDALL Jenner fired back at trolls after she was slammed for the “inappropriate” dress she wore to a friend’s wedding.

The 26-year-old defended her decision to wear the risqué dress in the comments section of an Instagram post.

Kendall got fans talking when she attended friend Lauren Perez’s November wedding in a skin-baring Monôt dress.

Lauren shared a number of photos from the wedding in late December on Instagram, which showed the guests celebrating her nuptials with husband David Waltzer.

Shortly after the bride shared the photos, Keeping Up With the Kardashians fans took to the comments section to call out the model for dressing “inappropriately” at the wedding.

One person wrote: “Inappropriate outfit at a wedding @kendalljenner. I’m embarrassed for you. #cringe.”

However, another fan defended Kendall’s outfit by writing: “If the bride herself doesn’t gaf why do you care? relax.”

The bride defended her friend by responding: "Tell em. SHE LOOKED STUNNING AND I LOVED IT!”

CLAPPING BACK

Kendall then chimed in by revealing that she asked Lauren for her permission before she wore the daring dress to the wedding.

The KUWTK star told her haters: “@laurenperez obvi asked for your approval in advance too. We love a beach wedding."

Before rocking the sexy black dress, Kendall wore a more traditional teal silk shift dress while serving as a bridesmaid during the wedding.

In addition to Kendall, the wedding's guest list also included fellow model Bella Hadid, Fai Khadra and Jesse Jo Stark.

ENGAGEMENT RUMORS

Kendall addressed her critics as some fans believe she may be engaged to boyfriend Devin Booker, 25.

The snap sparked speculation that the pair might be engaged, with many fans rushing to the Instagram post's comments section to ask if the pair had tied the knot.

One posted: "He's wearing a wedding ring?"

Another asked: "Anyone else notice the ring on his hand???"

A third fan wanted to know: "Ummm… did they get married or what?"
