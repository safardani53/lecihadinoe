Kylie Jenner accused of shamelessly regifting cake to makeup artist friend The US Sun

KYLIE Jenner has been accused of "shamelessly" regifting a free cake to her make-up artist friend.

One of her attentive followers noticed that a sweet treat the 22-year-old billionaire had sent to pal Desi Perkins looked exactly the same to one she'd put on her Instagram Story previously.

Posting on her social media yesterday, Kylie shared a photo of two boxes, tagging bakery Little House Confections to say thank you.

The gifts had arrived with a personalized note to the mother-of-one congratulating her on the European launch of her skincare range.

The second photo showed a close-up of the company's 'Bomb A** Olive Oil Cake', as well as a selection of their colorful cloud bombs.

Just two hours later, pregnant Desi, 33, took to her own social media channel to thank Kylie for some gifts that'd been delivered.

The stand out piece was a white rose flower arrangement that took the shape of a woman with a baby bump, with Desi admitting that she was "crying" at the "thoughtful" gift as she publicly thanked Kylie.

However, it was her second post that raised eyebrows, with Desi sharing a snap of the exact same cake that Kylie had received two hours earlier alongside the caption: "Also! Thank you for the sweets. I'm about to eat this whole thing in one sitting."

Sharing screenshots of both Stories to Twitter, a shocked fan wrote: "I'm not saying I was born to be a private detective but what I AM saying is Kylie Jenner regifted a charity cake."

Little House Confection's website states that proceeds from the sale of the Olive Oil Cake will be donated to health care workers in Los Angeles.

Responding to the tweet, one person wrote: "Noticed this too! Absolutely shameless although suppose less waste n all that."

Neither Kylie or Desi have responded to the speculation surrounding the cake.
