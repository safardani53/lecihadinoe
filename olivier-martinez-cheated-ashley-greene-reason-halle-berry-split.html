Olivier Martinez Cheated With Ashley Greene? The Reason For Halle Berry Split? Hollywood Life

As photos surfaced of Ashley and Olivier in the parking lot of a Bristol Farms Market in Los Angeles, Ashley was quickly labelled a “homewrecker” by the website sandrarose.com. Ashley and Olivier were photographed picking out pumpkins on Oct. 29, though it’s unclear what their connection really is. According to a source close to the actress, there is no connection. “This couldn’t be farther from true. She’s in a longterm relationship” an insider told HollywoodLife.com. So rest easy guys, Ashley didn’t break up Halle and Olivier.

Ashley is still very much together with her boyfriend of two years, Paul Khoury. On Oct. 22, she posted a photo to her Instagram of a gorgeous floral display with the caption “When your man comes home with flowers he arranged himself….And they’re perfect. #nofilternecessary love you @paulkhoury.” showing her love and appreciation for the Australian media personality.

As we previously reported, Halle and Olivier’s marriage has been in trouble for quite some time. “Olivier and Halle haven’t lived under the same roof for about a year,” a source told HollywoodLife.com EXCLUSIVELY. “They have been living separate lives, she has been in the family home, while he has been renting in an upscale apartment.”

“For the past year, both Olivier and Halle have tried to make it work for the sake of her first born, Nahla, who is very fond of Olivier and he doted on her,” the insider went on. “To make the transition easier for Nahla, and to seem like nothing was happening even though they were on the outs, they organized family days to make it appear to Nahla that a split wouldn’t change how often she’d see Olivier. And that’s the plan for the future, Halle won’t limit Olivier‘s time with Nahla, and obviously their son Maceo, moving forward.”
