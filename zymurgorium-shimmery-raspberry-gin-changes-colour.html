You can now buy shimmery raspberry gin that changes colour as you drink it

WE DON'T know about you, but our gin obsession is still going strong.

We've tried everything from mermaid gin to glittery bubblegum-flavoured gin - and now we've found a colour-changing raspberry option.

  • Flagingo Electric blue & Scottish raspberry gin-based liqueur, £19 from Zymurgorium - buy now

Available to buy from Zymurgorium, the brand calls the drink an "electric combination of blue and Scottish raspberries".

A gin-based liqueur, the description reads: "By adding a sparkling mixer to the swirling-shimmering blue madness, it triggers a wonderful on-stage explosion of pink hues."

In short, adding a tonic will turn it from blue to pink. Very cool.

Not only that, but it has a magical shimmery finish.

The gin will set you back £19, which is pretty reasonable in comparison to other novelty tipples.

However, you'll have to add on £7 for delivery.

Is it gin-credible enough to splash out on? We reckon so.
