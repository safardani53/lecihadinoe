Comic Book Author Tatsuki Fujimoto was born on October 10, 1992 in Nikaho, Japan (He's 31 years old now).

Japanese manga artist known for his dark fantasy mangas Fire Punch and Chainsaw Man. He collaborated with Oto Toda on the 2022 one-shot web manga Just Listen to the Song. Examples of his artwork are featured on his tatsukii.fujimoto Instagram account, where he has gained upwards of 150,000 followers.
