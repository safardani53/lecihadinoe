The End of the Line

The Potomac housewives have proven on several occasions that they can be a toxic bunch, but nothing could prepare viewers for the crazy drama that ensued when one a new cheating allegation emerged. 

When The Real Housewives of Potomac Season 2 Episode 14 got underway, the husbands made their way to the stage, and things got strained pretty quickly. 

Karen’s husband Ray was quizzed about their move from Potomac. If you watch The Real Housewives of Potomac online, you will know that everyone has been questioning the move. 

The couple then went after Charrisse for making comments about their finances. In case you missed it: Charrisse wanted everyone to think the couple’s finances were in dire straits. 

Charrisse then made everyone laugh when she called Ray "The Broke Bill Gates." Say what you want about Charrisse, but there’s no denying she is one of the wittiest women in reality TV. 

We then switched gears to Juan talking about wanting a life away from Robyn. This was the moment we’d been waiting for all season long. He failed to reveal whether there was a future for him and Robyn. 

Instead, he tried to dodge the question and looked shady in the process. The ladies revealed they were not impressed with the way Juan spoke about the relationship. 

They felt like he was messing with her emotions, and that’s not a good thing to be doing the person you are supposed to love the most. 

Another marriage was on the rocks, and we got the revelation that Ashley and Michael had been living apart for three months. The ladies picked up on Ashley trashing the other marriages, yet hers was falling apart. 

Ashley revealed that she had moved out of the house, but was adamant about finding her way back to Michael. However, he seemed to care more about them staying apart. 

The attention then switched to Charrisse, who revealed that Eddie was dating another woman and their marriage is over. Charrisse seemed more concerned with being named "The Grand Dame of Potomac."

Charrisse then shocked everyone by saying that Karen had a secret boyfriend and was lying about everything. We then hear the man was a security guard at her party, but she did not confirm the rumors. 

In the end, the ladies were divided, but they stressed that they wanted to work on things as they should be more unified as the Real Housewives of Potomac.
