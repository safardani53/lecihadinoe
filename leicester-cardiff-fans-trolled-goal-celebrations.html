Leicester fans trolled for 'celebrating like Wii sports characters' during 2-0 win over Cardiff

LEICESTER CITY fans have been trolled for “celebrating like Wii sports characters.”

The subdued celebrations came as the Foxes beat Cardiff 2-0 yesterday.

Goals from Kiernan Dewsbury-Hall and James Justin saw Leicester extend their lead at the top of the Championship table to eight points.

And Jamie Vardy’s side are looking good to seal automatic promotion back to the top flight.

However, some footy viewers could not resist poking fun at Leicester fans’ celebrations in the Welsh capital.

Defender Justin scored an absolute cracker in the 55th minute to put the second-tier game to bed.
