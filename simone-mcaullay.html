Simone McAullay Biography

Information related to her love affairs, boyfriend, married life and her divorce is unknown in the media.

As a child, Simone dreamed to be a dancer and she learned Ballet and Jazz classes from her young age. She studied dancing till she was 12 years old. With the completion of her high school graduation, she joined Curtin University but she dropped out from there without earning a degree from there. In the late 1990s, she joined Actors Centre in Sydney and she graduated from there in 2000. The following year she appeared in the TV series Crash Palace playing the role of Angie McIntye. She also made her feature film debut in 2001 by starring the role of Mrs. Harrison in a 2001 fantasy / martial arts film Invincible. The actual figure about her net worth and salary is unknown.
