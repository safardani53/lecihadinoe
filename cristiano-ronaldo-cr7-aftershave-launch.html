Cristiano Ronaldo launches new CR7 aftershave as he hits the decks at launch party in Madrid

CRISTIANO RONALDO has continued to cash in on his fame by launching his new CR7 aftershave.

The Real Madrid star has already made a fortune after entering the toiletries market with two signature smells.

And now he has added to the growing range with the new eau du toilette aimed at his huge following around the world.

Ronaldo took to the music decks at the launch party in Madrid last night, timing the release with the re-start of La Liga following the international break.

The CR7 is the latest in the line of fragrances, joining the Legacy and Legacy Private Edition scents in the market.

The fragrance is blended with notes of bergamot, cardamom, lavandin, tobacco, cinnamon, sandalwood, musk and vanilla.

And the makers claim the new offering is a 'contemporary cocktail of bold, daring scents, reflective of Ronaldo's strong, vibrant character'.

Ronaldo said: "It's my secret weapon. I start my day with CR7 fragrance and it gives me the confidence to live each day to the full"

More from football

The 32-year-old is one of the most marketable celebrities in the world on the back of his global fame from playing football.

And he is building a business portfolio away from the sport as he begins to near the end of his brilliant career.

Last year Ronaldo opened up his own CR7 hotel on his hometown island of Madeira, and has stakes in other luxury sites around the globe.

His multi-million pound sponsorship deal with Nike brances out beyond just football kit, while his underwear range has also proved a bid success.

He has even ventured into heavy industry after revealing he has invested in Egyptian steel because it is more environmentally friendly.

And he has also launched his own fashion brand offering a range of shirts and shoes.
