Moment huge brawl breaks out at Wireless Festival as men and women batter each other

THIS is the moment a huge brawl breaks out at Wireless Festival as men and women batter each other.

Revellers dressed in bright outfits can be seen trading blows in the terrifying confrontation at the festival in Finsbury Park yesterday.

Young men were filmed kicking and punching each other as shocked festival-goers watched the violence unfold on Friday.

Girls can be seen trying to get in between the fight - with one with purple braids appearing to kick one of the boys in the chest.

Witnesses could be seen trying to pull some people apart as the brawl intensified.

Security then rushed to desperately trying to control the scene.

After the confrontation, one of the boys is seen lying on the floor, appearing to be in pain after being struck.

The video was posted online yesterday, with the witness describing it as his "highlight of the day".

Tens of thousands of people descended on Finsbury park in London today to see performances from great acts including Cardi B, Travis Scott and A$AP Rocky, although he could miss the event due to an arrest in Sweden.

It comes after police released warnings for festival-goers, warning them to stay safe.

Met Police have been contacted for comment.

Festival organisers have also been contacted.
