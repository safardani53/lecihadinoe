Daniel Radcliffe goes nude for new film Jungle after revealing gruelling diet plan saw him lose two

DANIEL Radcliffe famously stripped off for his role in the play Equus back in 2008.

And now the actor treats fans to a glimpse of his bare bum in a cheeky scene from his new survival drama Jungle.

The 28-year-old strips off alongside his co-stars Joel Jackson and Alex Russell in the flick which is set in the Bolivian jungle.

The former Harry Potter actor has certainly shed his boy wizard alter ego in recent years, and his latest film is no exception.

Another racy scene sees Daniel romp with topless co-star Natasha Pruchniewicz.

The 28-year-old actor plays real life survivor Yossi Ghinsberg, who was stranded in a remote part of the Amazon rain forest for three weeks in Jungle.

But before he had to immerse himself in the dramatic and dangerous situations Yossi ended up in, Daniel does get some moments of respite and enjoys one particularly sexy scene with his gorgeous co-star.

Daniel recently denied claims he survived on a hard boiled egg a day while filming his challenging new movie.

While the Harry Potter actor fully immersed himself in the role of a wannabe adventurer who gets stranded in a remote part of the Amazon rain forest, he didn't resort to such drastic measures when it came to food.

Appearing on Lorraine, Daniel denied he ate a hard boiled egg a day when asked by the host.

He said: "No, I don't know where this has come from.

"I had a fillet of white fish or like a chicken breast and a protein bar a day for the two weeks before the scene."

That doesn't mean the 28-year-old over-indulged either, with him adding: "Then for the scenes where I was supposed to be very, very thin I didn't eat for two days but it is not a healthy thing to do.

"I don't advise anyone to do it, this is not the way to lose weight healthily but because I was doing it for a short period of time it was kind of OK.

"No one asked me to do it, it was sort of self inflicted but I think it was worth it."

In the movie - which is based on a true survival story - Daniel plays Yossi Ghinsberg, who survived three weeks in a remote part of the Amazon jungle back in the 1980s.

While Daniel admitted he isn't really a method actor, he felt he had to be true to Yossi's story, and it took a while to get back to normal after filming was finished.

Lorraine said: "I bet your girlfriend was glad when you started eating properly again?"

Daniel - who has been dating Erin Darke since 2012 - said: "She was very happy, she was a little worried about me.

"It took me a while to get back to eating properly afterwards because it does mess with your head a bit."
