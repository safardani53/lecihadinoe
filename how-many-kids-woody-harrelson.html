How many kids does Woody Harrelson have?

WOODY Harrelson is known as an American actor and playwright most associated with his role in Sony's Venom franchise.

When Harrelson is not working on a new movie, he can be found at home with his wife and kids.

Who are Woody Harrelson's kids?

Over the years, Harrelson has tied the knot twice.

He was married to Neil Simon's daughter, Nancy Simon, from 1985 to 1986.

Following their divorce, Harrelson went on to start a relationship with Laura Louie, whom he wed in 2008 in Hawaii.

The couple have three daughters together, including Deni Montana, 29, Zoe Giordano, 26, and Makani Ravello, 16.

Harrelson's oldest daughter, Deni, has managed to stay out of the spotlight for most of her life aside from a few rare red carpet appearances with her dad.

Zoe has also lived a quiet life but has dabbled in the fine arts. She appeared alongside her father in the 2015 short film, U2: Song for Someone.

That same year, she also hosted a Ted Talk at her high school where she talked about the importance of “[disconnecting] from phones,” which, according to the video's description, are “removing us from reality and moments."

While her older sisters have tended to stay out of the spotlight, Makani has already started her career as an actress and has appeared in two films so far.

Makani made her acting debut in 2016's LBJ as a crowd member. She then landed the role of Deni in the 2017 movie, Lost in London.

Did Woody Harrelson get into a bar fight?

On October 7, 2021, it was announced that Harrelson was involved in a physical altercation at the Watergate Hotel in Washington, where he was reportedly working on filming a new series for HBO.

Harrelson allegedly hit a man in an act of self-defense after getting into a "verbal dispute," according to a police report.

Apparently, the man had taken a picture of Harrelson and after he "approached the man and requested him to delete the photos," according to an article published in The Washington Post, the man then lunged toward Harrelson in an attempt to grab his neck.

At this time, it is unclear if any charges were filed.

Who was Woody Harrelson's dad?

Growing up, the girls never really got the chance to know their grandfather because of his criminal record.

Harrelson is the son of notorious American hitman and organized crime figure Charles Voyde Harrelson , who is most famously known for assassinating federal judge John H. Wood Jr. in 1979.

Following the assassination, Charles was sentenced to two life sentences and later died in prison at the age of 68 after a heart attack.

In 1988, Harrelson opened up about his father and revealed that he didn't really consider him a father figure.

"I don't feel he was much a father," Harrelson told People at the time. "He took no valid part in my upbringing."
