I was fed up with my staircase wall being so dull so gave it a jaw-dropping transformation and it on

A SAVVY home interiors fan has revealed how she transformed her boring wall on a budget - and all you need is a £6 buy.

For a while, crafty TikTok user Mary B. (@mehree.b) was fed up of her dull staircase wall and had wanted to give a more modern and luxe feel.

But instead of painting it or hanging some art, she decided to cover the white wall with multiple mirrors - and they were nabbed from her local Target.

The woman, from the US, took to TikTok to share the jaw-dropping house glow-up, inspiring thousands of people across the world.

In total, Mary showed in her video, she has purchased 15 mirrors with black framing, each one of which cost her $7 ($6).

The stunning mirror wall with black accents gave the wall a whole new look, whilst also creating the illusion of a larger space.
