Kardashian critics mock Kylie Jenner's 'obnoxious' new outfit &amp; compare it to sister Kim's infamous

KARDASHIAN critics have mocked one of Kylie Jenner’s new dresses and compared it to an infamous outfit once worn by Kim.

The dress in question was one of many divisive looks that the youngest KarJenner sibling showcased recently at Paris Fashion Week.

While in the French capital, one of 25-year-old Kylie’s dresses in particular caught attention from fans.

She made a dramatic entrance to the event on Wednesday wearing a floor-length white gown by Acne.

While the bodice of the dress closely hugged the mom-of-two’s curves, the sleeves and the skirt dramatically dropped down to the ground.

In fact, the dress was long that Kylie had to be helped out of her car and onto the red carpet so she would not step on the dress.

The Kylie Cosmetics boss appeared to love the outfit as she posed for a series of snaps in her hotel room which she posted to Instagram on Monday.

In one shot, she emphasised the dress’s dramatic drape by posing with her arms outstretched to the walls.

However, fans on social media were forthcoming with their disapproval of the look.

One reshared the image of Kylie with her arms outstretched and wrote: “Me putting on a fitted sheet.”

Others compared the look to a piece of ravioli, and even a fried egg.

“It’s giving failed attempt at a toga party,” wrote another, while one slammed Kylie as being “self indulgent”.

THE ‘COUCH’ DRESS

The dress reminded some of Kim’s infamous 2013 Met Gala appearance, for which she wore a skin-tight floral number.

However, the outfit quickly became a meme as fans compared the look to a “couch”.

Kim, who was heavily pregnant at the time of the event, recalled in a 2019 interview with Vogue that she was left “crying" due to the online reaction.

THESE STREETS ARE MADE FOR WAKLING

However, the white dress was not the only controversial look that Kylie rocked while in Paris.

On Sunday, the Hulu star wore a futuristic ensemble by Balenciaga to watch the designer’s show.

She was photographed in a fuzzy hot pink dress that some fans compared to a character from Sesame Street.

Kylie was also accused of wearing “the worst footwear ever” as she stepped out on Sunday wearing bright blue boots with a cut-out for her toes.

Fans likened the social media queen to a “Smurfette” and “Daisy Duck” in her Balenciaga pair.

Kylie has now landed back in Los Angeles after turning the streets of Paris into her own personal fashion show.
