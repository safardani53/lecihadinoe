Kristen Wiig Goes Nude For New Movie: Check Out the Pics!

Last night, Kristen Wiig appeared on The Tonight Show as the Khaleesi from Game of Thrones and gave a hilariously inept interview to Jimmy Fallon.

But while she may not know much about Daenerys Targaryen, Wiig certainly knows what makes the Internet tick (i.e. Game of Thrones and nudity).

All she has to do now is take a funny photo with a cat and she’ll have hit the trifecta:

That’s Kristen going full frontal in her new movie Welcome to Me, and this is by far the most unexpected nudity we’ve seen in quite some time.

As revealed least night during Kristen’s hilarious Khaleesi interview, the film is about a woman who wins the lottery and uses the money to finance her own daytime talk show.

Hey, this isn’t the first time someone has used nudity to drum up publicity for an ill-fated daytime TV venture. (Remember Kris Jenner’s talk show? Neither does anyone else!)

Wiig has certainly entered an interesting stage in her career and whether she’s performing with Sia at the Grammys or starring in a Lifetime movie with Will Ferrell, she definitely has a knack for keeping us guessing. 

Also, we hate to take it here, because it looks like stills above are from a dramatic scene, but we’d be doing you a disservice if we didn’t discuss the elephant (or tiny dragon, as it were) in the room: Kristen looks damn good naked!

So there you have it: The first review for Welcome to Me is an enthusiastic thumbs up rave from THG!
