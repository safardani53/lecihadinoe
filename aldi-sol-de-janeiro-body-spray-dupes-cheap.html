Aldi shoppers sprint to get their hands on new 2.99 Sol De Janeiro dupesthey're 19 cheaper &amp;

UNLESS you’ve been living under a rock recently, it’s highly likely that you wil have heard of Sol De Janeiro.

It’s a beauty brand that’s become hugely popular this year, with the iconic ‘Bum Bum cream’ and the summer sprays plastered all over social media.

But if you can’t justify spending £22 on a body spray, fear not, as Aldi have come to the rescue with their brand new dupes.

And not only are the spray dupes a whopping £19 cheaper, but according to beauty fans, they smell ‘exactly the same’ too.

One Aldi shopper named Becca spotted the cheap dupes when shopping in her local Aldi and was eager to share them on social media. 

The 20-year-old posted a clip online, showing off the new-in body mists.

Becca said: “Don’t walk, run to Aldi for the Sol De Janeiro spray dupes.” 

Numbered just like its OG Sol de Janeiro counterpart, there’s three different scents available, all of which are just £2.99 a pop.

The first scent that Becca spotted, is Lacura’s Thirty Two Havanna Nights, which she claimed “smells like 62.”

She then found the Sixty Four Tropical Glow, which she explained “smells like 40.”

Finally, Becca got her hands on Twenty Eight Cuban Dreams, which she believed “smells like 68.” 

The budget-friendly alternative sprays offer a lightweight, long-lasting fragrance and each scent is built on a base of vanilla and musk, and embellished with a range of fresh, fruity and floral notes.

At just £2.99 each these will set you back less than your average Starbucks order, but you’ll need to move fast if you want to nab one. 

Becca later added: “Mad at myself for not buying any.

“They smell exactly the same!”

Becca’s TikTok clip, which was posted under the username @becaaelizabeth, has clearly impressed many, as it has quickly gone viral and in just one week, has amassed a staggering 5.4 million views.

It has 575.8k likes, 10.7k comments, 101.9k saves and 15.7k shares.

Social media users were stunned at the iconic dupes and many were desperate to get their hands on them. 

One person said: “OMGGG IM RUNNING.” 

A second chimed in: “I’m sprinting with my asthma puffer in my hand.”

Another added: “I want.”
