TODAY star Dylan Dreyer has playfully taunted her fellow co-host Jacob Soboroff in an awkward live TV moment.

Dylan made a joke about Jacob while teasing an upcoming segment on the show.

Style expert and host Chassie Post appeared on the morning show to give tips on finding the best online shopping deals.

Kristen Welker, who filled in for Today stars Hoda Kotb and Savannah Guthrie, teased: "Chassie Post joins us this morning with the best of the best when it comes to online shopping, including a festive outfit that's perfect for New Year's, you don't wanna miss that."

The camera panned to Chassie showing off mannequins wearing black turtlenecks paired with sparkly gold and silver skirts.

"I can see you in that Jacob," Dylan said while Chassie flaunted the outfits.

Jacob took a pause and responded: "You know what? I was thinking the same thing Dylan Dreyer."

"It's your color," Kristen added with a laugh.

Dylan frequently shows off her sense of humor with her co-hosts, including Craig Melvin.

'HOT MESS!'

Craig recently roasted Dylan after she admitted to getting "anxiety" before throwing parties.

Dylan said: "I do get anxiety when I host a party. I get like a stomach ache... social anxiety.

"But I get that when I go to a party anyway."

Craig turned to his co-host and said, grinning: "You're a bit of a hot mess."

Sheinelle Jones, who was sitting next to Craig, couldn't resist joining in on the teasing and did an impression of Dylan before a party.

Imitating Dylan acting nervous, she said: "She's like, 'I love throwing a party.

"'And then I get sweaty and clammy and my stomach hurts… so great! I get to cook and make plates and put Post-it notes.'"

Craig cracked up laughing and added: "Here's what else she'll do.

"She'll complain about it at work, before and after the party."

The presenter hosted a bash at a bowling alley and shared a sweet snap with her husband, Brian Fichera, and their three sons from the day.

Dylan had baby Russell, one, in a sling around her neck and carried Oliver, two, on her hip.
