Brad Pitt and Daniel Craig lead A-list stars at Wimbledon final

BRAD Pitt and Daniel Craig have led the A-list stars who showed up to watch the Wimbledon final.

The stars were out in force as they flocked to Centre Court to watch Carlos Alcaraz face Novak Djokovic.

Huge names made appearances on day 14 at the at the All England Lawn Tennis and Croquet Club.

Brad Pitt led the pack as he was seen looking tense whilst watching the highly-publicised game.

The 59-year-old actor wore gold rimmed aviator sunglasses whilst he sported a turquoise polo-neck t-shirt under a matching overshirt.

Daniel Craig, 55, was also present at the highly-anticipated match alongside his actress wife Rachel Weisz, 53.

The pair put on a chic front as the former James Bond star donned a textured grey suit with a striped tie and a bold red pocket handkerchief.

Rachel meanwhile sported a delicate beige silk blazer with matching trousers and a high buttoned white shirt.

Elsewhere in the court, Hugh Jackman, 54, looked smart as he was seen in a navy blue suit and a white t-shirt.

He was accompanied by his wife, Australian actress Deborra-Lee Furness, 67, who wore a cream coloured ensemble to Centre Court.

Meanwhile, US megastar Ariana Grande, made a surprise, but also low-key appearance at the tournament.

She sported a white Wimbledon branded cap on top of her bleach blonde hair which was styled into light curls.

The Break Free hitmaker was joined by fellow actors Andrew Garfield and her Wicked co-star, Bridgerton actor Jonathan Bailey as they all enjoyed refreshing cocktail drinks.

The megastar kept her look casual as she donned a casual grey knitted jumper and light makeup.

Both Jonathan Bailey, 35, and Andrew Garfield, 39, donned different shades of sand coloured suits as they chatted to the recording artist.

Meanwhile Tom Hiddleston, 42, was spotted just one row in front of the group as he opted for navy coloured blazer with a complimentary suit and tie.

This was after Emma Watson, 33, Sex Education and Dr Who actor Ncuti Gatwa, 30, and Stormzy, 29, all posed together in the Evian VIP Suite.

Creed II actress Tessa Thompson, 39, was also seen quickly making her way into the venue in a floral dress and a light camel coat.

Whilst Lily James, 34, cheered from the stands as she sported a navy blazer on top of an off-white striped shirt.

This comes as all the names are facing extended down time amid the Hollywood actors strike.

Ariana is among those who has downed tools on the two-movie adaptation of Wicked, despite it being just days from finishing.

It’s a major headache for film studios, as it could be months before the 160,000 striking actors return to work.

The stars’ union, the Screen Actors Guild, announced the strike on Thursday night after failing to reach a deal over fair pay from streaming services and an agreement that artificial intelligence wouldn’t replace actors.

Not only does the action stop actors from working on set, it also prevents them promoting their films.

Brad's own Formula 1 film, Apex is one of the films that has been affected, alongside Ryan Reynolds and Hugh Jackman’s Deadpool 3, and the live-action version of Snow White starring Rachel Zegler.

Margot Robbie attended a photocall in London for her Barbie movie on Thursday — dressed in a pink two-piece.

Whilst the cast of Oppenheimer including Florence Pugh and Cillian Murphy had to walk out of the premiere in the capital on Thursday night.
