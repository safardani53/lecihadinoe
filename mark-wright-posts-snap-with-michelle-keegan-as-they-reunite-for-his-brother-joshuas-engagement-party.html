Mark Wright posts snap with Michelle Keegan as they reunite for his brother Joshua's engagement part

MARK Wright has posted a sweet snap with his actress wife Michelle Keegan at a party to celebrate his footballer brother Joshua's engagement.

The pic of the TV couple, who've been married since May 2015, follows the news that Michelle refuses to send nude pics or videos to Mark for fear that the iCloud will be hacked.

The pair cuddled up at Joshua's Hawaiian-themed engagement bash, which featured a cardboard cut-out of Josh proposing to Hollie Kane.

And loved-up Mark, 30, couldn't help enjoying the company of his ladywife, who he gets to see very rarely due to her overseas filming commitments with BBC's Our Girl.

Mark captioned the pic: "Amazing night celebrating the bro @joshwright4444 and future sis in law @kanehollie engagement.

"Love yas x #bringonthewedding."

The last time Mark posted a picture of himself with Michelle on Instagram was way, way back in March 2017.

So he looked pleased as punch to have Michelle with him in the flesh, seeing as neither of them engage in nude snap swapping while apart for long periods of time.

Yesterday former Corrie star Michelle confirmed that they do not message each other racy pictures or clips.

The 30-year-old has learnt from other stars’ naked picture leaks over the years that technology is not to be trusted.

When asked by the Daily Star if they exchange nudes, she exclaimed: “We wouldn’t do that.

“People hack the iCloud!”

Mark and Michelle have been married for over two years now, but due to her working in South Africa for Our Girl, and his international DJ and presenting gigs, the pair have been constantly plagued with rumours of relationship crisis.

However the actress insists that everything is “fine” between her and the former Towie hunk.

She said: “Mark and I are very good and everything is fine.

“Our marriage is going really well. Me and Mark know that I have to go away for work. He’ll come visit me and it’ll be fine.”

Pointing out the double standards between men and women’s careers, she adds: “Why can’t a woman work away and have a separate career from her husband and still have a good marriage?

“That’s the frustrating thing. What do people think goes on? Everything is fine with us. We have separate careers. Why is it a bad thing?”

In the past Michelle has been pictured not wearing her wedding ring after removing it for filming and keeping it in a safe place, however she now pays not attention to the “haters and trolls.”

She said: “I speak my mind and it gets me into trouble. It’s so negative these days. I’m just a normal girl. I’m just doing my job. I don’t really take notice of the haters or trolls, I really don’t.
“I don’t read them. So no matter what they say, I don’t really care. I don’t feel the need to prove anyone wrong. I have chosen the career I want for myself. I don’t need to prove anything to anyone.”
