Kendall Jenner shows off red micro thong in throwback after she was slammed for promoting 'unrealist

KENDALL Jenner flaunted her perfect figure in a throwback photo wearing a teeny tiny red micro bikini despite being slammed for promoting "unrealistic body standards."

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star faced backlash for sharing the images last Valentine's Day as many believed them to be "photoshopped."

Kendall, 26, took to Instagram on Sunday to share a few throwback photos in honor of the holiday of love.

In the first picture, the reality star posed on a raised pink platform in front of a matching curtain wearing just a red bra and micro thong bottoms.

The TV personality let her long brunette locks lay loose around her shoulders and she finished off the skimpy look with clear pointed-toe heels.

Kendall captioned the re-share: "Vday tomorrow" as she reminisced from her last year's shoot.

In the second post, the supermodel squatted down to show off her perfect physique and six-pack abs.

The video documented the TV star as she moved back and forth for the camera, the wind machine blowing her hair.
