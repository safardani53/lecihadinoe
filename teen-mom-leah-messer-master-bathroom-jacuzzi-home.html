Teen Mom Leah Messer takes fans inside her master bathroom featuring a deep jacuzzi tub in $500K Wes

TEEN Mom Leah Messer has given fans a glimpse at her luxurious bathroom and spa setup inside her West Virginia home.

The reality star took to Instagram on Thursday to share a video of the space.

In the clip, Leah, 31, focused the camera on her lavish jacuzzi tub filled with bubbles and foam in her master bathroom.

Other beauty products lined the tiled ledge of the tub, along with flowers, a candle, and a romance novel.

The tranquil scene continued with plants hanging and a small window to look out of while in the bath.

Leah completed the setup with a tray table over the tub that included a candle, dry brush, more beauty items, and her memoir, Hope, Grace, and Faith.

Before putting on her cozy white slippers, she zoomed the camera in on each product, which she highlighted in her caption.

The Teen Mom 2 star wrote in part: "I'm obsessed with this Lavender Dream Spa package that I received from @spaluxetiqueofficial right in time for Mother's Day!"

Leah then shared a discount code and encouraged her followers to "treat yourself" with the at-home treatment.

She set the video to the song Good Energy by Yung Wylin'.

LAVISH PAD

Leah first showed off her $500,000 home last April after announcing that she moved into the mansion with her then-boyfriend, Jaylan Mobley, 25.

The Kanawha County Clerk's Office exclusively confirmed to The U.S. Sun that Jaylan bought the house for Leah on March 31.

According to the Ashley Reality Roundup, Jaylan's new 6,000-square-foot home he's sharing with Leah cost him just under $499,900.

The brick exterior home contains five bedrooms, four bathrooms, and a massive basement.

It features a basement family room, a game room, and a sauna.

Other features of the home built in 1994 include a large outdoor deck, laundry room, and a two-car attached garage.

The Charleston property sits on .37 acres of land and has enough space for her daughters - twins Ali and Aleeah, 13, and Addie, 10 - to have their own rooms.

Leah posted a video on Instagram of her and Jaylan revealing the home to the girls for the first time.

Along with the girls' adorable shocked reactions, the interior of their home has high ceilings.

Other areas have clean and smooth wood flooring.

As the episode aired, Leah tweeted, writing: "This doesn't have anything to do with WHY we broke up but the deed scene was BS as well. He made up a fake document to get my real on-camera happy reaction and had me believing I was on the deed until we got home and I learned otherwise."

She included the hashtags "#PublicityStunt" and "#FOH."
