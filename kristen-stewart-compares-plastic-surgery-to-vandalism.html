Kristen Stewart Compares Plastic Surgery to &quot;Vandalism&quot;

Kristen Stewart thinks Kylie Jenner is totally and completely nuts.

The reality star has finally confessed to having lip fillers in her very plump lips – and Stewart has made no secret over how she feels regarding any sort of surgical enhancement.

“I am so freaked out by the idea of doing anything," she says in the latest issue of Harper’s Bazaar U.K., explaining:

"And maybe that’s completely arrogant but I don’t want to change anything about myself. I think the women who do are losing their minds. It’s vandalism."

The only thing the former Twilight Saga star likes to do more than not smile is share her opinion.

In this same magazine piece, she goes on about how Hollywood is disgustingly sexist, saying it’s "crazy" and "offensive" how "women inevitably have to work a little bit harder to be heard."

Give K. Stew credit, however: she is making her voice heard on a number of controversial topics.

Gotta admire her for that, don’t you?
