Who is Guru Gobind Singh:

Guru Gobind Singh is a famous Religious Leader. He was born on December 22, 1666 and his birthplace is India. Guru is also well known as, Sikh leader and philosopher who served as the last of the ten Sikh gurus. He is responsible for the popularization of the Sikh practice of covering of one’s hair with a turban and passed Guruship of the Sikhs to the Eleventh and Eternal Sikh Guru, the Guru Granth Sahib Ji.

Guru is originated from India. His father was Guru Tegh Bahadur, the ninth Sikh guru.
