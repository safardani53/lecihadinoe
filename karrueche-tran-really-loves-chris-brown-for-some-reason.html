Karrueche Tran Really Loves Chris Brown For Some Reason

In honor of Valentine’s Day, Karrueche Tran has let the world know that she has terrible taste in men.

The D-Lister took to Instagram on Saturday in order to express her affection for the man who beat Rihanna to a bloody pulp, writing of Chris Brown:

“Happy Valentine’s Day to my love.. You drive me crazy and you’ve made me crazy but that’s just us I love you just as much as you love trying to match my outfits.” 

In the affiliated photo, Brown is grabbing his crotch and flipping off the camera.

Seems about right, doesn’t it? Who could NOT love that face?!?

Back in December, Brown told concert goes that he’s "single" and added in reference to Tran: "F-ck that b-tch." He later accused her of cheating on him with Drake.

A day later, Brown apologized and expressed his love for his long-time ladyfriend.

Since then, engagement rumors have actually surrounded the couple, especially after Tran flashed a diamond ring for the world to see on social media.

Finally, last month, Brown serenaded Tran in his “Autumn Leaves” music video.

Where is this relationship headed? We have no idea. But we’d have 911 on speed dial if we were Karrueche Tran.
