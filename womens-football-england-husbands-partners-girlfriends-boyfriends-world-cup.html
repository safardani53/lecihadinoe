Meet the HAPs (thats husbands and partners) wholl be cheering on Englands Lionesses during

THE entire nation will be cheering on the England women's football team this World Cup.

But also shouting words of encouragement from the sidelines in their match against Scotland at France's Stade de Nice on Sunday are the players' supportive partners.

Just like the WAGs of England's male team who became crucial for their calm and constant presence at the World Cup in Russia last year, the HAPs are set to play just as important a role this summer.

From captain Steph Houghton's ex-footie husband Stephen Darby, to Toni Duggan's boyfriend Tony and Jodie Taylor's wife Emma Kete - who is playing for New Zealand - we take a look at the loved ones who could help the team to victory.

Steph Houghton's husband Stephen Darby

The 31-year-old - who is captains both the Manchester City and England teams - got married last June to Bolton Wanderers right-back Stephen Darby.

Shortly after their wedding, he was diagnosed with motor neurone disease.

It was revealed in September that he would be retiring at the age of 29 from football.

However, despite this illness, he is expected to travel to France to watch his wife play.

Toni Duggan's boyfriend Tony

The Barcelona player - who earlier this week said the Lionesses should not be paid as much as their male counterparts - regularly shares loved-up snaps of her and boyfriend Tony on her Instagram.

Last month, the 27-year-old sportswoman shared a pic of the couple before what appeared to be a glam date night, and wrote: "Mi Amor."

Just weeks beforehand, she shared another selfie with her man, and revealed: "Can’t wait to see you!"

In February, she enjoyed a romantic Valentine's Day dinner for two with him in the Spanish city where she is winger or forward.

In July 2017, when she became the first England player since Gary Lineker to be signed to Barcelona, she thanked "my amazing boyfriend" for his support.

Leah Williamson's boyfriend Mason Childs

The 22-year-old star, who plays for Arsenal, is dating Mason Childs, 22.

Just like Toni's man, he  appears on her Instagram - which boasts 113k followers and stylish campaigns for Swarovski and Nike.

Last year, she wished him a happy 21st birthday, and also indicated that the pair met as children playing football.

Beside old and current snaps of them, she wrote: "My boy from daydot is 21.

"So grateful for the love and support you have given me travelling all over. Can’t wait for many more memories."

Jodie Taylor's wife Emma Kete

The striker for Reign FC, who is currently on loan to Melbourne City, recently revealed that her other half, Canberra United player Emma Kete, will be heading to France to represent New Zealand.

Shortly afterwards, Emma posted a pic of the couple clapping hands and wrote: "Same dream, different team. I get to play at a World Cup with my wife."

The 31-year-old frequently shares sweet pics of her and Jodie, 33, on her Instagram.

Emma captioned a cosy black and white pic located in Tacoma in the United States : "Mrs & Mrs."

Alongside another adorable pic taken in Seattle, she wrote: "Home is where the heart is."

Ellen White's husband Callum Convery

The Manchester City forward, 30, is married to Callum Convery, a sports development officer for the Football Association.

It appears she has no competition from her husband, with whom she lives in Nottingham, on the pitch.

"He used to like playing soccer. Now, if we have a knock around together, he usually ends up in goal,” she told The Sunday Times.

Alex Greenwood's boyfriend Jack O'Connell

The 25-year-old Manchester United player has been dating Sheffield United defender Jack O'Connell, 25, for several years after meeting at school.

With the football season having ended Jack could very well be making the trip to France.

Rachel Daly's girlfriend Kristie Mewis

The defender, 27, who plays for Houston Dash in Texas, is dating girlfriend and American teammate Kristie Mewis, 28.

She regularly shares loved-up snaps of the couple on Instagram, and is believed to be already en route to France to show her support.

Millie Bright's boyfriend Jordan Bird

Millie, 25, is dating 25-year-old Jordan Bird, who works as a First Assistant on the Pam Barton Course Royal Mid-Surrey Golf Club.

He regularly gushes about the Chelsea defender on social media.

Sharing a promotional video on Twitter recently, he wrote: "Love it. keep smashing it mushy."

Lucy Staniforth's girlfriend Laura Wilson

The attacking midfielder, 26, for Birmingham City is in a relationship with Laura Wilson.

Her girlfriend, an inclusion officer for Coventry City, is believed to have already jetted out to cheer on her other half.
