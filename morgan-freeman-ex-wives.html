Who are Morgan Freemans ex-wives?

ACADEMY Award winner Morgan Freeman has been married twice during his career.

As of the time of writing, the veteran actor who is well-known for his distinct voice has four children.

Who are Morgan Freeman’s ex-wives?

During his early days, Morgan Freeman tied the knot with Jeanette Adair Bradshaw on October 22, 1967. However, they divorced after more than a decade of marriage on November 18, 1979.

After their divorce, Jeannette kept a low profile and stayed out of the limelight.

Subsequently, Morgan went on to marry Myrna Colley-Lee on June 16, 1984. The couple later separated in December 2007 and officially divorced on September 15, 2010.

Myrna Colley-Lee had a successful career working as a costume designer for some of Hollywood's biggest productions.

How many children does Morgan Freeman have?

As of the time of writing, Morgan Freeman has four children - two named sons Alfonso and Saifoulaye, as well as daughters Deena and Morgana.

Speaking on his life as a father, he told the Telegraph: "It took a while for me to become a father.

"I was busy trying to be an actor, and the two didn’t really go together in my youth."

He continued: "Now, I am in my dotage. I am a great-grandfather. I’m not a hands-on grandfather.

"I haven’t changed diapers. But when I had daughters, I changed theirs."

What is Morgan Freeman's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Morgan Freeman has an estimated net worth of approximately $250million, as of December 2023.

His main source of income is reportedly from his career as a veteran actor, director, and narrator.

He has also earned income from brand endorsements and sponsorships.
