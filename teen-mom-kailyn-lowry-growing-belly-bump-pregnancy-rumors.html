Teen Mom Kailyn Lowry spotted with growing belly bump in T-shirt and sweats outside studio as pregna

TEEN Mom star Kailyn Lowry has been spotted with a growing belly bump in a T-shirt and sweatpants outside of her Delaware podcast studio, as pregnancy rumors continue to swirl. 

Fans are speculating Kailyn, 31, is pregnant with twins nearly a year after The U.S. Sun exclusively reported the Teen Mom 2 alum secretly gave birth to her first child with her boyfriend, Elijah Scott, in November 2022. 

In photos exclusively obtained by The U.S. Sun, Kailyn is seen leaving her Baby Mamas No Drama podcast studio on Wednesday, September 27. 

In the photos, Kailyn’s growing belly bump is on full display despite the star having yet to confirm the potential pregnancy.

Kailyn is wearing a football T-shirt that reads, “Game day, game day, game day” and sweatpants. 

The star had a serious expression as she exited the studio.

PREGNANT AGAIN?

The shocking new photos come after The U.S. Sun obtained exclusive photos of the star leaving her Delaware podcast studio on Thursday, September 7 with a belly bump. 

In the photos, Kailyn flaunted her stomach in a tight black tank top and gray biker shorts.

The photos from this month are a contrast from images obtained by The U.S. Sun on May 24, when Kailyn attended her six-year-old son Lux’s baseball game. 

Kailyn wore a black shirt and matching leggings, as a small stomach bump could be seen in the photos.

In another set of photos taken earlier in the day, the podcast host covered her bump with a red zip-up hoodie and blue sweatpants while leaving her podcast studio.

Back in March, Kailyn showed off her slim figure in a bikini selfie.

If the twin pregnancy rumor is true, Kailyn is going to be a mother to seven children. 

She is mom to son Isaac, 13, who Kailyn shares with ex-boyfriend Jo Rivera, Lincoln, nine, with ex-husband Javi Marroquin, and Lux and Creed, three, with ex Chris Lopez. 

Her youngest son Rio with her boyfriend Elijah was born on November 20, a source previously confirmed to The U.S. Sun. 

PREGNANCY CLUES

The speculation that Kailyn is pregnant with twins began in May when she promoted vitamins on her Instagram Stories.

Followers noticed that she wore a bracelet on her left wrist that read "mom of seven."

An Instagram account called @TM_chatter reposted screenshots from the video on their profile, writing: "Confirmed. #KailLowry is pregnant with twins. She will be the mom of 7. You’re welcome."

But Kailyn seemingly denied the rumors, like she did with her pregnancy with Rio, in a TikTok video with Elijah, who she began dating in 2022. 

In the six-second clip, Kailyn lip-synced to a quote about rumors, saying: "Ever heard a rumor about yourself and actually wanted to hear more? Like, 'damn, what did I do next?'" 

Fans also started to notice a change in Kailyn’s appearance, as she had a swollen nose, which is a sign of pregnancy. 

One fan commented regarding her appearance: "She's preggo for sure you can see it all in the face.”

A second agreed: "Her nose always gives it away."

A third said: "And that face is spreading love like it does every time she’s pregnant!”

A fourth agreed: "Why deny the pregnancy? Her face tells on her every single time."

The MTV alum also appeared to show off her supposed baby bump while on vacation in Cancun, Mexico over the summer.

She has also refrained from posting full-body shots in recent weeks. 

SECRET BIRTH

The pregnancy speculation for her fifth son Rio began in July 2022 when Kailyn’s ex Chris, whom she has a tumultuous co-parenting relationship with, tweeted: "Out here talking bout you'll beat me up now you know you gotta protect the belly stop it."

In that same month, The U.S. Sun obtained pictures of Kailyn looking tense outside of her Delaware home with Elijah. 

Fans claimed to have spotted a bump in the photos, as one commenter posted on Reddit: "Oh My God, Kailyn pics! Is she pregnant?"

Then in September, The U.S. Sun obtained more unedited images of the star with an apparent bump leaving a Wawa convenience store in Delaware. 

A month later, Kailyn was photographed running errands at Target and Hobby Lobby with her boyfriend. 

In the photos, Kailyn wore a baggy graphic T-shirt with black leggings, as she covered her stomach with the oversized top. 

In November, fans noticed a bump during the star’s late-night Wawa run with Lux in photos obtained by The U.S. Sun. 

One fan wrote on Reddit: "Kailyn spotted out! Looks like a bump to me.”

A second said: "I’m about 90% sure she’s pregnant.”

A third remarked: "She’s definitely pregnant. Anyone trying to pretend like she isn’t is just fooling themselves."

Then in January, after The U.S. Sun's insider claimed she gave birth, Kailyn appeared to have lost weight, as she showed off a slimmed-down figure. 

The U.S. Sun revealed photos of the podcast host wearing a loose-fitting graphic T-shirt with black pants and sneakers for an outing. 

SOCIAL MEDIA CLUES

Throughout the supposed pregnancy, Kailyn posted photos on social media either cutting off or covering up her stomach.

When Kailyn did share full-body images, she wore baggy hoodies and shirts.

For the last two weeks of November, Kailyn took off from her Baby Mamas No Drama podcast, as fans speculated she could be on maternity leave.

Just over a week after the source revealed she gave birth, fans were convinced they could hear the sound of a newborn baby in the background of one of her social media videos.

Also in December, fans noticed what looked like a baby car seat in the back of her video.

Then, Chris went off on his baby mama in another heated rant on Instagram that read: “You claim to be so real so raw but yet you got a whole newborn you should be focused on and yet you're trying to create unnecessary drama to cover up that FACT.”

In July, Kailyn finally confirmed she welcomed her fifth child despite denying the pregnancy.

Kailyn told The Culpo Sisters star Aurora Culpo on her podcast Baby Mamas No Drama that she watched her series when she was in the hospital giving birth.

She said: "I first came across your show... I was in the hospital having one of my kids. The first episode that I saw was, I believe, you told your parents that you were, maybe, separating or divorcing and then one of your sisters was going to watch your kids.”

The Culpo Sisters premiered in November 2022.
