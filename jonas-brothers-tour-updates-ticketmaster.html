What did Nick Jonas and Priyanka Chopra name their baby?

On April 20, 2022, TMZ reported that the pair’s baby girl, who was born just after 8pm on January 15 in San Diego, was officially named Malti Marie Chopra Jonas – according to the birth certificate obtained by the outlet.

The couple announced on January 21, 2022, that they welcomed a child via surrogacy.

“We are overjoyed to confirm that we have welcomed a baby via surrogate,” Jonas, 29, wrote in an Instagram post.

“We respectfully ask for privacy during this special time as we focus on our family.”

How tall are the Jonas Brothers?

Each member of the Jonas Brothers is known for their unique personalities and sense of humor.

The oldest sibling, Kevin Jonas, is reportedly also the tallest in the band, rolling in at around five feet and nine inches.

His wife, Danielle, is around five foot five.

Joe Jonas comes right in the middle, standing at five feet and seven inches. He’s notably shorter than his wife, Sophie Turner.

Sophie’s about five foot nine.

Nick Jonas is the youngest member of the band, and also reportedly the shortest. He comes in just under Joe, standing at about five feet and six inches.

Priyanka Chopra, his wife, is about five foot five.
