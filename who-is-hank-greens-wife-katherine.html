Who is Hank Green's wife Katherine?

THE PAIR have been married since 2006.

On Friday, May 19, 2023, successful vlogger Hank Green revealed his cancer diagnosis.

Who is Hank Green's wife Katherine?

Katherine Green was born on May 17, 1980.

The 43-year-old has appeared in her husband's YouTube vlogs and, according to Showbizcorner, helps him with his gaming channel and enjoys playing Nintendo's Super Mario Bros.

She also plays The Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword, as per Nerdfighteria Wiki.

The outlets also note that Katherine is a script supervisor for Crash Course Chemistry.

Katherine is also popular on Twitter, where she has over 25K followers on her page, @realkgreen.

Katherine and Hank, 43, got married in 2006 and live in Missoula, Montana.

On September 20, 2011, Hank shared a video commemorating the couple's fifth university to @vlogbrothers, the vlog he shares with his brother, John Green.

As he shared in the clip, the pair had traveled to the San Juan Islands in Washington to celebrate the occasion.

Katherine revealed that the pair dated "for a long time" before tying the knot.

Do Hank and Katherine Green have kids?

The couple have one kid together: A six-year-old son named Orin Green, who was born in October 2016.

Hank has referenced his son on social media.

On March 19, 2021, the author shared a series of photos of him and his wife — taken by Orin — to Instagram.

He wrote in the post: "Katherine and I went to this spot a little over four years ago but we didn’t have anyone to take a picture of us.

"Today Orin took his first intentional picture of someone besides himself, and I think he did great. Swipe for outtakes."

On March 27, 2021, Hank tweeted about a sweet interaction with Orin in which the child said: "I need help with I’m alone."

What did Hank Green say about his cancer diagnosis?

On Friday, May 19, 2023, Hank uploaded a video to Vlogbrothers in which he shared that he has been diagnosed with cancer, specifically Hodgkin’s Lymphoma.

The entrepreneur revealed that he spoke to his doctor after noticing that his lymph nodes were enlarged.

Following the diagnosis, he shared that he will be starting chemotherapy "very soon" and that it seems like they caught his cancer in the early stages.

He explained that Hodgkin's Lymphoma is "super treatable" and that "the goal is cure."

Hank then revealed: "My first treatment begins....drumroll please, literally as this video goes live. So, there's a very good chance that I'm reading the comments right now while getting my first round of chemotherapy.

"I've also gotten my PET/CT, which showed that the cancer has not spread anywhere from its original location in my left armpit/chest area, which is very good news.

"This sucks so bad, but I'm already learning so much...mostly about myself."

He concluded the video by stating: "I hope that this isn't my last Vlogbrothers video for four months or whatever.
