KOURTNEY Kardashian has stripped down to just her silk pajamas during a cozy night in after giving birth to her son Rocky.

In early November, the Poosh founder gave birth to her fourth child Rocky Thirteen, her first with her husband, Travis Barker, 48.

On Monday, Kourtney, 44, took to her Instagram Stories to share her New Year's Eve outfit with fans.

In the photo, The Kardashians star was wearing a pair of cream-colored silk pajamas and NYE costume glasses.

She captioned the post: "A perfect, cozy New Year's Eve in pajamas."

Kourtney sported a trio of silver necklaces in the photo.

One had a charm that read "Mrs. Barker," while another featured the name of her newborn son, "Rocky."

Also on Monday, Kourtney teased her post-baby body in a full-length pic as she supported her husband as he ran a marathon in Santa Monica, California, over the weekend.

In a group photo shared by one of Travis' friends, the Hulu star dressed warmly and in all dark colors in the middle while posing with a kissy face.

The Lemme founder also stunned in a pair of knee-high boots and a fur jacket.
