Kendall Jenner poses butt naked in ultra-sexy Instagram snap following sister Kylie's Snapchat hacki

KENDALL Jenner has set Instagram on fire by posting a naked portrait of herself sprawled on a stone table with her legs in the air.

The 21-year-old reality star, who held a cigarette in the saucy picture, cheekily captioned the snap "i don't smoke" - clearly thinking her naked form needs no further comment.

The willowy supermodel sister bagged nearly two million likes on the snap within three hours of posting it, rivalling even her younger sister Kylie's Instagram popularity.

Harry Styles' ex, who's used to bagging her most likes through selfies and saucy Snapchats, looked quite similar to model Emily Ratajkowski in the black and white snap.

The picture attracted a range of comments from her 82 million followers, including "wow", "Stunning" and "DAMN GURL".

However, some of her fans were less than impressed that she had posed with a cigarette when she doesn't smoke them.

One blasted: "Why do you take photos with cigs if you don't?"

Another wrote "You don't smoke but you like to smell like them? I don't understand", while a third said she was a "bad influence" for "glamourising" the habit.
