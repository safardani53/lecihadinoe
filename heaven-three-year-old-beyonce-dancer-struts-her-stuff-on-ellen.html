Three-Year-Old Beyonce Dancer Struts Her Stuff on Ellen!

The toddler who danced her heart out to Beyonce and became a viral phenom has taken her fancy footwork to – where else – Ellen DeGeneres’ Show!

Heaven (a.k.a. "two-year-old dances to Beyonce" on Google) showed that she’s picked up a thing or 20 from her mother, who’s a dancer and choreographer.

Her videos, posted by mom, racked up hundreds of thousands of views, and after her appearance on Ellen, that number will likely climb. Big time.

Heaven’s awesome, on and off the dance floor!
