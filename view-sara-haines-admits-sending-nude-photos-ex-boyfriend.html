Although this is the first time Sara has opened up about something like this, she hasn't shied away from NSFW topics before.

Earlier this month, Sara and Sunny made a sexual joke in an NSFW live TV moment.

During the final segment of the show, the co-hosts were joined by Oprah Daily's creative director Adam Glassman.

The trio cycled through a number of beauty and skincare products that were immediately available to be purchased by the viewers.

Adam began by introducing a face roller made by his favorite facialist in Los Angeles, Nurse Jamie.

He demonstrated the product while narrating, "It uplifts. It massages your face, it tightens your face."

Sunny then asked: "Is it cold?"

To which Adam responded, "No, it is not cold...but these right here, these cryo-balls are amazing."

The creative director pointed to a pair of metallic orbs positioned on the other side of the display.

Sara checked them out and exclaimed, "Cold balls!" much to the delight of the audience.

She kept the joking rolling as Sunny took her turn demonstrating the product.

Sarah remarked, "You rub it on your face," accompanied by the continued chuckles from inside the studio.

Sunny told her to "check this out," which Sara did while giving the audience a knowing glance.

She let out a sensuous, "Oh yeah," before moving on to the next product.

OH, SARA!

Last month, Sara also made a NSFW joke to Joy, after talking about the Covid-19 booster.

The conversation peaked with Joy, never one to shy away from a risqué joke, to make a comment about her experience getting the shots to protect against the virus.

"I just got my third booster," she stated. "I've been jabbed more than I was on my honeymoon."

Her co-hosts and the audience erupted in laughter at the unexpected remark.

"I mean, I've had five shots," Joy continued while holding her hand to the crowd.

Joy went on to reveal she had recently received yet another booster, to which Sunny asked: "Did your arm hurt after?"

But before Joy could finish her answer, Sara surprisingly interjected, asking Joy: "What about the wedding night? Did you hurt?"

The out-of-nowhere quip brought the roof down, as everyone burst out in laughter, while Sara turned a bright red.

Sunny joked: "I didn't say that. That was the good girl from Iowa. I didn't say that," glancing over at Sara.

Amused by her co-host's remark, Joy chuckled: "She likes a callback."
