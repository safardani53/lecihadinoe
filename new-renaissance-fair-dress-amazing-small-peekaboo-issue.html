I'm 5'9&quot; and 210 lbs with DDs - my new Renaissance Fair dress looks amazing but there's a small peek

A FASHION-FORWARD woman has revealed her look for the much-anticipated Renaissance Fair.

She said although the dress fit her like a glove, one small issue may be a wardrobe malfunction waiting to happen.

Shaquel (@shaquel_the6figurechick) filmed herself modeling the frock in a video on TikTok.

Financially-savvy Shaquel typically creates videos about the job market, but started off by explaining why she felt the need to share her look.

"Now, I am fully aware this is not my usual content, but do you see this f**king dress?" she said.

"The Renaissance Festival is happening in Texas right now. I thought this would be a cute outfit to put on." she added.

She shared that she may need to add one tweak to the look to prevent a wardrobe malfunction.

"I'm going to have to wear some shorts," she said, showing that the back of the dress flowed over her backside a little too short.

She shared some of her measurements for reference of the fit of the dress.

"But y'all, I've got DDs. I am five-foot-nine and I weigh 210 pounds. And this looks f**king amazing. Look at this," she raved.

The piece is called the F is For Fairies Handkerchief Dress, by Dolls Kill and retails for $48.

"The cheeks are definitely out, but this is so cute. I can’t wait to show y’all a fully styled outfit," she added.

The video drew in many viewers who shared their thoughts in the comment section.

"I needs this," one wrote. "It’s giving Tinkerbell."

"Right? I’ve been seeing it and finally thought 'I might as well' and I’m so damn glad I did," Shaquel wrote back.

"You elven goddess," another wrote.

"New favorite thing to be called," Shaquel replied. "Adding it to the list now."

Renaissance Fairs occur globally as a theatrical way to recreate a fictional world where history meets fantasy. The fairs are inspired by the middle ages.
