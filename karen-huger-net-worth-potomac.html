Who is Karen Huger from Real Housewives of Potomac?

NICKNAMED the Grand Dame, Karen Huger is a member of The Real Housewives of Potomac.

The television personality has been on the show since its premiere in 2016.

Who is Karen Huger?

Born on May 3, 1963, Karen Huger is a native of Virginia.

She grew up on her family's farm and attended the University of Virginia.

Karen is also an entrepreneur, launching her signature fragrance, La'Dame, in 2018.

She sells wigs, candles, and home fragrances under the La'Dame name.

What is Karen Huger's net worth?

Apart from her husband, Karen Huger is estimated to have a personal net worth of approximately $10million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

On top of her various beauty and lifestyle brands, Karen is an ambassador for Promoting Awareness Victim Empowerment (PAVE) and the Alzheimer's Association.

She is vice president of the board of directors for Felicia's Fund, a nonprofit meant to help girls remain in school.

Karen boasts over 490K followers on Instagram and is available for public speaking opportunities.

Is Karen Huger married?

Karen married her husband, businessman Raymond Huger, in 1996.

Together, the couple shares two children: Rayvin and Brandon.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Raymond has an estimated net worth of around $40million.

He spent over two decades with IBM before launching Paradigm Solutions International in 1991.
