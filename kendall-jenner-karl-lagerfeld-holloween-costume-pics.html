Dresses Up As Sexy Icon Hollywood Life

Kendall may just win for most accurate impersonation outfit! The model, who is very close with designer Karl Lagerfeld, donned a perfect white wig with dark sunglasses when heading into a Halloween party with a friend. We have to say, under the jacket was the perfect look: a sparkly plunging unitard with black netting covering her cleavage and of course a black tie. She has the details down to a tee, wearing black fingerless gloves just like Karl. The bodysuit was actually a custom design by the amazing Shady Zeineldine.

Of course, to keep it sexy, Kendall paired the unitard with fishnets and knee-high boots, showing off her seriously long legs! We know that her sister, Kylie Jenner (dressed like a sexy Xena) was also in attendance at the party with her boyfriend, Tyga. However they had trouble getting out of their car because of paparazzi, TMZ reported.

Tyga reportedly got out of the SUV first, but Kylie was mobbed so bad when she tried getting out, the car left and went around back to let her in — leaving Tyga out front. Kylie clearly wasn’t happy. She later took to Twitter, to write “Paparazzi- lack of respect will get you no photos.”

Luckily, she did get inside and post a ton of Snapchats with Tyga, Malika Haqq and of course her sister.

So, do you like Kendall’s look more than Kylie’s? Do you think she rocked the perfect Karl? Let us know!
