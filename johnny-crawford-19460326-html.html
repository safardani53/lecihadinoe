John Ernest Crawford is an American actor, singer and songwriter. He first performed to a national audience as a Mouseketeer. At the age of 12, Crawford rose to fame for his role as Mark McCain in the ABC Western series The Rifleman, for which he was nominated for an Emmy for Best Supporting Actor at age 13 for his work in the chapter. Crawford had a brief career as a recording artist in the 1950s and 1960s. He continued to be active in television and film as an adult. Beginning in 1992, Crawford conducted the California-based Johnny Crawford Orchestra, a classical dance orchestra that performed at special events.
