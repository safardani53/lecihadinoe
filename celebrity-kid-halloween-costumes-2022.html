Celebrity Kid Halloween Costumes of 2022 [PHOTOS]

Lea Michele's Son, Leo

The Funny Girl Broadway star shared an adorable shot of her son Ever dressed up for Halloween. The photo, which was posted on Michele's Instagram Story, features the 2-year-old dressed in surgical scrubs and a doctor's white coat.

"Happy Halloween from me and my McDreamy aka Dr. Ever," she wrote over the photo, adding a pumpkin emoji.

Hoda Kotb's Daughters, Hope and Haley

The Today co-host took two princesses trick-or-treating this Halloween. Her daughters, Hope, 3, and Haley, 5, wore tulle dresses in pastel colors and toted decorative candy baskets to pick up sugary snacks.

In an Instagram video posted by Kotb, the girls stop into a nail salon hoping — and succeeding — to leave with some sweets. "Trick or treating new york style-- store fronts!" wrote Mom in the caption.

Olivia Munn and John Mulaney's Son

Munn and Mulaney didn't have to look far for their son's costume inspiration. The couple kept it in the family and dressed the 11-month-old as his maternal grandmother, Kimberly.

"Happy Halloween!! Here is Malcolm dressed as my mom," said Munn on Instagram. She and her comedian husband dressed their baby boy in a black wig, covered his lap with snacks and handed him an iPhone to hold. The X-Men: Apocalypse star shared a photo of her real mom as a point of comparison, and she provided a checklist of the necessary items that made Malcolm's costume so accurate.

"The sunflower seeds strewn about, Wheat Thins, extra long Slim Jims, flip flops, floral pants, novelty T-shirt, always watching Vietnamese YouTube on her phone," wrote Munn. She also posted a photo of Mulaney with his real and faux mother-in-law, adding, "She's also John's best friend."

Ciara and Russell Wilson's Daughter, Sienna

Halloween turned this mother-daughter duo into a set of sisters! The "One, Two Step" singer and her daughter Sienna, 5, dressed up to recreate Venus and Serena Williams' Got Milk advertisement. Ciara and her mini-me wore their hair in braids, dressed in all black clothes and added milk mustaches to the upper lips. The bottom of the photo, which was posted on Ciara's Instagram, includes an extra line celebrating the tennis legends: "To the best female athletes of all time! Venus and Serena, Love Ci Ci and Si Si."

Meghan Trainor and Daryl Sabara's Son, Riley

It was all cuddles and no claws for the "All About That Bass" singer and her son this Halloween. Trainor and 20-month-old Riley, whom she shares with husband Sabara, wore zookeeper and lion costumes to celebrate the holiday. In an Instagram photo posted by Trainor, the toddler looks cozy in a furry onesie with a lion's mane, eyes and nose on the hood.

Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott's Kids, Stormi and Baby Boy

The beauty mogul and rapper draped themselves and their two kids in all white this Halloween. The family of four went all out in angel costumes. Feather wings hung behind Jenner, her 4-year-old daughter Stormi and her 8-month-old son, whose name is yet to be announced. Stormi even topped off her heavenly attire with a fluffy halo headband.

Hilary Duff's Daughters, Banks and Mae

The Younger star celebrated a wild Halloween with her two daughters. On Instagram, Duff shared photos of Banks, 4, and Mae, 19 months, cozied up in animal-themed costumes. The youngest of the pack wore a fuzzy zebra-striped onesie with eyes and ears on the hood, and her older sister rocked a sleeveless leopard print outfit.

Teddi Mellencamp's Kids, Dove, Slate, Cruz and Isabella

The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills alumna brought together her family of six for a Top Gun-themed Halloween costume. The whole family wore the matching jumpsuits, including their daughters Dove, 2, and Slate, 9, their son Cruz, 6, and husband Edwin Arroyave's daughter Isabella, 12, from a previous relationship.

On Instagram, Mellencamp revealed that her team was less than enthused to don the matching getups. "They felt the need…the need to speed right out of these costumes after we got this pic," she joked. "Apparently, it's humiliating to wear matching outfits after you turn anything over 7."

Mandy Moore's Son, Gus

The This Is Us star may have hinted at her son's 2022 Halloween costume a year ago! For his first-ever Halloween in 2021, Moore posted photos of baby Gus dressed in a goose costume with the caption, "Duck, duck, GOOSE." This year, the now-20-month-old appeared to take on the other bird mentioned, although he didn't look too happy about it.

"Someone is NOT a fan of dressing up," Moore joked on her Instagram Story, where she shared a photo of a visibly displeased Gus wearing a brown duck costume, with tan and white wings attached to his arms and a green hat topped with an orange bill.

Jana Kramer's Kids, Jolie and Jace

"You've got a friend in us," the One Tree Hill alumna captioned an Instagram photo of her family group costume. The trio (plus their dog) dressed as characters from Pixar's film, Toy Story. The mother of two went as cowgirl Jessie, while her daughter Jolie, 6, was Little Bo Peep, and her son Jace, 3, looked fly in his Buzz Lightyear costume. Their pooch, Leo, wore a cowboy outfit (sans hat) to channel the movie's main character, Woody.

Mario Lopez's Daughter, Gia

Talk about spooky! The TV host's daughter, Gia, nailed her Pennywise costume this year. The 12-year-old wore a red wig in pigtails, which matched the color of her clown makeup, her red sneakers and the accents on her blue dress.

Tiffani Thiessen's Kids, Harper and Holt

The Saved by the Bell alumna and her family traveled back in time this Halloween. The four of them all opted for '80s- and '90s-inspired costumes. The family posed in their retro costumes for a group Instagram. Thiessen and her husband, Brady Smith, dressed as Ghostbusters characters Zuul and Vinz Clortho. Their daughter Harper, 12, dressed as Cher from Clueless in a yellow plaid skirt set and knee-high socks. Her brother Holt, 7, wore sagging pants, a chain around his neck and no shirt, making him the perfect Mark Wahlberg in his Marky Mark rapping era.

Jade Roper and Tanner Tolbert's Kids, Emmy, Brooks and Reed

This family of five had a super Halloween! The former Bachelor and Bachelorette contestants looked powerful as Superman and Wonder Woman. Their kids embodied different heroes as well. Sons Reed, 23 months, and Brooks, 3, went as the Hulk and Spider-Man, respectively, and their big sister Emmy, 4, dressed as Super Girl.

Bre Tiesi and Nick Cannon's Son, Legendary

"The legendary Mickey 🐭 wishing you all a very happy Halloween 🎃," wrote new mom Tiesi on Instagram. She shared several photos of baby Legendary Love, 4 months, dressed up in a full body Mickey Mouse costume complete with the cartoon's classic round ears atop the hood.

Tom Brady and Gisele Bündchen's Kids, Benjamin and Vivian

The Tampa Bay Buccaneers quarterback embraced the spooky season in full costume. As he accompanied his kids for some trick-or-treating, Brady's face was completely concealed under a grim reaper outfit. His son, Benjamin, 12, wore a light-up ensemble and coordinating helmet mask, while his daughter, Vivian, 9, dressed in a boxy red, orange and black costume.

Brady — who shares both kids with Gisele Bündchen, from whom he announced his divorce Friday after 13 years of marriage — discussed his plans for this year's Halloween on his Sirius XM show, Let's Go!

"Hopefully I'm somewhat anonymous tonight so the kids can really enjoy themselves," he said. "It could get a little hectic and if it does then I'll come back home. But I'm hoping I can be just another dad out there on the street tonight in a cool costume."

Shawn Johnson East's Kids, Drew and Jett

This year, the former Olympic gymnast stuck the landing with her family's costume coordination. The household of four (or five, including their Golden Retriever) dressed up as characters from Disney's 2013 animated musical Frozen. According to an Instagram shared by Johnson East, who dressed as Anna, her husband Andrew East went as Kristoff, their daughter Drew, 3, went as Elsa and their 15-month-old son, Jett, was Olaf.

Even the family's dog, Nash, got into the holiday spirit. He donned antlers to be Kristoff's pet reindeer, Sven.

Tori Spelling's Son, Beau

The Beverly Hills, 90210 alumna shared a photo of her son Beau, 5, wearing a ninja costume this Halloween. In one photo posted to Spelling's Instagram Story, Beau smiles with a ghost decoration in hand. Another sees the youngest of five asleep behind his mom, who snapped a selfie while laying on the bed. "Now my little ninja naps before the madness begins this eve 🎃," Spelling wrote over the picture.

Heather Rae and Tarek El Moussa's Kids

Only on Halloween could you find a cowgirl, two sharks and a pregnant skeleton in the same Instagram photo. The kids — both of whom HGTV's Tarek El Moussa shares with ex Christina Hall — looked festive and fun. Taylor, 12, wore a jean skirt and a cowgirl hat, and Brayden, 7, matched with his dad in shark costumes.

Heather's baby bump got in on the fun too. The Selling Sunset star, who revealed her pregnancy to PEOPLE in July, wore a black skeleton outfit that even featured a skeleton baby on the stomach.

Heidi Montag and Spencer Pratt's Son, Gunner

The reality stars' 5-year-old, Gunner, celebrated Halloween as an orange tyrannosaurus rex. While Pratt followed his son's Jurassic lead theme in a blue pterosaur costume, Montag wore a tiara and a pink dress. In a TikTok video posted by the expecting Mom, she called her outfit a "pregnant queen" costume and joked that her main goal was not to go into labor" while on the trick-or-treat route.

Ndamukong Suh's Sons, Kingston and Khari

The NFL star, his wife Katya and his twin boys went for a Disney favorite this Halloween. The family of four dressed up as characters from 101 Dalmatians. Mom and Dad went as Cruella DeVil and Roger Radcliffe, and their 19-month-old toddlers, Kingston and Khari, suited up in spotted onesies topped with floppy ears, blue eyes and puppy noses.

Drew Scott and Linda Phan's Son, Parker

Baby Parker spent his first Halloween matching with his mom and dad in matching pumpkin costumes. The Property Brothers star shared an Instagram Reel of his 4-month-old sitting adorably in his plush patterned outfit handmade by his maternal grandmother.

"Parker's first #Halloween! He must've stayed out past midnight and turned into a pumpkin!" Scott captioned the post. "Thanks to Linda's mom for the cutest costumes 🎃❤️."

Neil Patrick Harris and David Burtka's Twins, Harper and Gideon

Once again, the spooky season revved the Burtka-Harris family's creative spirit. This year, the quartet dressed up as some of America's famed fast-food faces. In an Instagram shared by Harris, who dressed up as Ronald McDonald, the four mascots sit covered in various servings of fried food, with empty food wrappers strewn about.

Burtka wore a crown and embellished coat for his Burger King costume, while the 12-year-old twins, Harper and Gideon, were Wendy of Wendy's renown and KFC's Colonel Sanders.

Vanessa and Nick Lachey's Kids, Camden, Brooklyn and Phoenix

For their family costume, the TV star couple wore prison jumpsuits while their three kids posed as mini NCIS agents. The NCIS Hawaii actress shared a few photos of the group on Instagram, captioning the post with some whole appreciation for their chosen Halloween ensembles.

"Orange you glad we escaped!" Lachey joked before sharing the heartwarming backstory. "Seriously though, when Brooklyn said she wanted to 'be like Mama' my heart melted! I've always dreamed of this moment, but never knew what it would feel like. I will cherish this forever!"

Witney Carson's Son, Leo

"Happy Halloween from Cruella and her dogs 🐶🎃🌙," wrote the Dancing with the Stars pro on Instagram, where she shared a photo of her family of three all dressed up in 101 Dalmatians-inspired garb.

Carson wore a black and white wig, black dress, red gloves and a white fur wrap for her Cruella DeVil costume, while her husband, Carson McAllistar, and their 21-month-old son, Leo, matched in Dalmatian onesies.

Maralee Nichols and Tristan Thompson's Son, Theo

As seen on her Instagram Story, the fitness model dressed as Little Red Riding Hood, and 10-month-old Theo complemented her look in his Big Bad Wolf costume.

Andy Cohen's Daughter, Lucy

This year, the Bravo star celebrated his first Halloween as a father of two. Cohen ventured out for some trick-or-treating with his kids. For her first Halloween, 6-month-old Lucy dressed as a tiny princess in a blue gown and tiara headband.

Andy Cohen's Son, Benjamin

The TV personality and his son Benjamin, 3, both wore skeleton jumpsuits as they hunted for candy around their New York City neighborhood.

Brittany and Patrick Mahomes' Daughter, Sterling

For this year's Halloween, the Mahomes shone as a family of three (and soon to be four!). Each dressed as a bright feature of the night sky. The NFL quarterback wore all black under a giant sun costume, and his wife, who co-owns the Kansas City Current, attached a cutout of the moon over her baby bump. Their daughter Sterling, 20 months, was the star of the show in metallic bellbottom pants and a shiny gold star costume.

"The Sun, the Moon & the beautiful Ster✨🤍 #happyhalloween," wrote Brittany Mahomes, captioning an Instagram post of the trio.

Jessica Alba and Cash Warren's Son, Hayes

Big or small, every truck needs its pit crew! This Halloween, Alba and husband Cash Warren's son Hayes, 4½, dressed up as a monster truck, and his parents came along as his mechanics.

"This monster truck 🚙🛻 and his mechanics 🛠️🛞 are trick-or-treat ready👻🎃," the actress captioned a series of Instagram photos taken of coordinated costumes.

Jenna Bush Hager's Kids, Mila, Poppy and Hal

The Today co-host shared a few festive photos of her family dressed up for the spooky holiday. Her Instagram showed Bush Hager, husband Henry Hager and their three kids in a variety of costumes.

The TV personality and her daughter Poppy, 7, in bewitching black outfits accessorized with pointy flat-brimmed hats. The couple's 3-year-old son Henry (Hal for short), matched with Dad in green dinosaur suits. Their eldest, Margaret "Mila," 9, went for an all-white cat costume embellished with face-painted whiskers, cat ears and a bowtie.

Hilaria and Alec Baldwin's Kids, Eduardo and Maria

The New York-based couple hit the city streets for trick-or-treating with their entire pack. While newborn baby girl Ilaria snuggled into a hooded pink onesie, her siblings rocked five different costumes between the six of them. The toddler team of Eduardo, 2, and Maria, 19 months, went as "baby spider people," as Mom dubbed their matching Spider-Man costumes on her Instagram Story.

Hilaria and Alec Baldwin's Son, Romeo

Romeo, 4, the true middle child of the seven siblings, dressed as Pikachu, wearing a full yellow jumpsuit to match the Pokémon character.

Hilaria and Alec Baldwin's Son, Leonardo

In a fur-textured mask and an animal-like top with broad shoulders, 6-year-old Leonardo went as "some sort of creature," according to his mother's caption on social media.

Hilaria and Alec Baldwin's Son, Rafael

The couple's eldest son, Rafael, wore all black to be "some sort of scary demon," wrote Hilaria alongside a photo of the 7-year-old holding a trick-or-treat bag and a candy wrapper. The mom of seven also wrote that he already "ate like half his candy." She added, "Sugar high is real."

Hilaria and Alec Baldwin's Daughter, Carmen

The Baldwin duo's firstborn, Carmen, 9, nailed her cheeky costume. She donned an all-brown ensemble, from the poop emoji hat on her head to the matching slippers on her feet.

Hayley and Tyler Hubbard's Kids, Olivia, Luca and Atlas

Florida Georgia Line singer Tyler Hubbard, his wife Hayley and their kids enjoyed a magical halloween together. The family of five dressed as characters from Peter Pan, with Dad as Captain Hook and Mom as Tinkerbell. Their eldest, Olivia, 4, wore a Wendy Darling costume, while their sons Luca, 3, and Atlas, 2, were Peter Pan and Michael Darling, respectively. They even had their dog dress as Nana, the Darling family's bonnet-wearing pooch.

"Of course I got everyone's costumes together and realized I forgot my own," wrote the mother of three on Instagram, where she shared photos of their costumes. "So an extra thanks goes out to Amazon for completing our family Halloween fun this year, in record time."

Princess Gabriella and Prince Jacques of Monaco

These royal twins put their own spins on classic Halloween looks. Princess Gabriella wore a pumpkin dress and matching Jack-O-Lantern headband, while her brother, Prince Jacques, sold his vampiric costume with fake blood and a cape.

Their mother, Princess Charlene, shared a photo of her spooky 7-year-olds on Instagram, writing, "Happy Halloween," in the caption.

Mario Lopez's Kids, Dominic and Santino

Access Hollywood host Mario Lopez shared a clip of himself and two of his three kids on his daily show this Halloween morning. Lopez and his sons, Dominic, 9, and Santino, 3, dressed in matching costumes as the Three Amigos.

As a mariachi band played behind them, Lopez and his eldest introduced the show with a performance of the trio's salute dance from the 1986 film, and Santino held his hat and watched adorably.

Nicole 'Snooki' Polizzi's Kids, Lorenzo, Gio and Angelo

Jersey Shore alumna Nicole "Snooki" Polizzi shared all three of her kids' costumes to social media on the morning of Halloween. Her oldest son Lorenzo, 10, wore a skeleton suit and matching makeup, and her daughter, Giovanna, 8, looked furry and fabulous in a werewolf outfit. Polizzi's youngest, Angelo, 3, wore all black and white to be an orca whale.

The reality star also shared a cheeky glimpse into her Halloween morning mom duties on her Instagram Story. "My ass was doing skeleton makeup at 6am getting yelled at by my kids like I'm suppose to be a professional face painter," wrote Polizzi alongside a selfie of her holding up a peace sign.

Kim Kardashian's Daughter, North

A few days after 9-year-old North West posed as Aaliyah for a group costume with her siblings, North continued the musical theme when she and her friends dressed up as R&B trio TLC. On the TikTok account she shares with her mom Kim Kardashian, North showed off her Rozonda "Chilli" Thomas costume and danced to the group's hit "No Scrubs."

Donald Faison's Daughter, Wilder

Donald Faison reprised his role in Clueless this Halloween, when he and his daughter dressed up as Murray and Dionne from the 1995 film. In a black-and-white skirt set, a pink top and an elaborate headpiece, Wilder, 7 nailed the character's iconic look (which was worn by actress Stacey Dash in the movie).

Donald Faison's Son, Rocco

On Instagram, Donald Faison also shared a solo shot of his son, Rocco, 9, dressed as Allen Iverson. The Scrubs alum shares his youngest two kids, Rocco and Wilder, with wife CaCee Cobb.

Ashley Tisdale's Daughter, Jupiter

High School Musical alumna Ashley Tisdale's daughter wore a precious llama costume on the day before Halloween, while Mom and Dad went as a zookeeper and a safari guide. Tisdale's other Instagram stories from the day revealed that her family wasn't the only one to dress in animal style: She tagged a number of fellow parents, including Meghan Trainor and Hilary Duff, in a photo of the wild bunch of toddlers playing outside.

Bindi Irwin's Daughter, Grace

The Irwin family brought The Office to Australia this October. Bindi Irwin and her husband, Chandler Powell, dressed in halloween costumes once worn by characters Jim and Pam, while her brother, Robert Irwin, went as their fictional co-worker Dwight. The youngest of the group, Irwin and Powell's 19-month-old, Grace, wore a "World's Best Boss" T-shirt for her Michael Scott costume.

Abby De La Rosa and Nick Cannon's Twins, Zion and Zillion

"'Toto, I've got a feeling we're not in Kansas anymore.' #halloween2022 #wizardofoz," said Abby De La Rosa on Instagram, where she posted an adorable Instagram Reel of herself and her sons in costume. The former radio personality shares twins Zion and Zillion, 16 months, with Nick Cannon.

Scheana Shay and Brock Davies' Daughter, Summer

Vanderpump Rules star Scheana Shay and her family looked Pixar-perfect in their Halloween costumes this year. The trio dressed up as the main characters from Monsters Inc, with Shay as Mike Wazowski, Davies as Sulley and 18-month-old Summer as Boo. "Monsters Inc. & Boo 👧🏻," wrote Shay on Instagram, sharing a group photo one night before the spooky holiday.

Khloé Kardashian and Tristan Thompson's Kids, True and Baby Boy

On Oct. 30, Khloé Kardashian gave followers the tiniest peek at her new son in 4-year-old daughter True Thompson's arms. "Owlette and Tigger aka True and Baby Brother. (Shhhhh…. But I can't wait for Halloween to be over)," the newly minted mom of two posted on Instagram.

Carrie Underwood's Son, Isaiah

Days before Halloween, Carrie Underwood shared a video of her oldest son, 7-year-old Isaiah, dressed as an old man.

"I need an old man dance, give me an old man dance," the singer said in the video before her son started to perform his imitation of the routine. "This kid!!!!" she also wrote in the caption.

Kim Kardashian's Kids, North, Saint, Chicago and Psalm

In anticipation of Halloween, the KKW Beauty founder posted glamorous photos of her four kids dressed as hip-hop and R&B legends of the '80s and '90s. "THE ICONS - AALIYAH, SADE, SNOOP, EAZY E," Kardashian wrote in her Instagram caption.

In homage to the late singer Aaliyah, 9-year-old North wore a full Tommy Hilfiger outfit — from her tube top to her belt to her navy-and-red pants with a pair of Hilfiger-branded boxers peeking out — just like the "Princess of R&B" wore in her 1997 campaign video for the fashion line. Meanwhile, Chicago, 4, dressed as Sade in an outfit inspired by a striking portrait of the singer. She channeled the British songstress perfectly in double denim and chunky gold jewelry with her hair in a hip-length braid.

Saint, 6, and Psalm, 3, looked spot-on as rappers Snoop Dogg and Eazy-E, respectively. The youngest of the group wore a black "Compton" hat indistinguishable from the one worn by the late Eazy-E on several of his album covers. Saint took on Snoop Dogg's classic plaid button-down look with natural ease.

Khloé Kardashian and Tristan Thompson's Daughter, True

The 4-year-old wore a sleek cat costume during a pre-trick-or-treat outing, matching cousin Dream Kardashian in an all-black outfit complete with bright colored face paint. Instead of wearing faux ears, True's hair was styled into two tiny top knots on either side of her head.

A few selfies posted by mom Khloé Kardashian, who appeared to be chaperoning the cousins and a friend, showed that the whole group dressed up for the spooky event in the days leading up to Halloween.

Blac Chyna and Rob Kardashian's Daughter, Dream

The reality stars' 5-year-old wore black head-to-toe during her Halloween adventure with cousin True Thompson. Dream's costume included shiny pants, a tank top, black cat ears and face paint to top off her feline look.

In a series of Instagram stories shared by her aunt, Khloé Kardashian, Dream seemed like she was completely immersed in the full fall spirit of the holiday. The girls posed by a pumpkin-filled "graveyard" and a wall decorated with giant jack-o'-lanterns.

Tori and Zach Roloff's Kids, Jackson, Lilah and Josiah

TLC star Tori Roloff shared some adorable photos of her kids all dressed up ahead of Halloween. The eldest Roloff mini, Jackson, 5, donned a full firefighter costume. His little sister, Lilah, 2, opted for a dalmatian costume that included a floppy, spotted dog-ear headband and a tutu.

The newest addition to the Roloff family, Josiah, looked too cute in a hatchling costume. The 5-month-old wore a fluffy hood with a chick's beak and eyes while the rest of him nestled into a cracked eggshell getup. He even had webbed yellow slippers on his feet!

Josiah sells the scene in an Instagram post from his mom, leaning up against a chicken coop with a bird just behind him.
