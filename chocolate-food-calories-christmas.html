We reveal how many 'bite-size' treats add up to full-size calories

YOU may think you can munch away on chocolate mini-bites this Christmas – but don’t be fooled by their size.

Just a few tiny treats can have a BIG impact on your waist.

If you wolf three Heroes Fudges you will have eaten more calories than in a standard bar.

Here, AMY JONES and KATE JACKSON reveal the shocking full-size and mini chocs’ calorie and fat counts, plus their equivalent prices, and tell you how few little ’uns match the calories of a big ’un.

Fudge

Full size: 25.5g
Calories: 114
Fat: 3.9g
Price per 100g: 98p (based on 25p cost of bar)

Mini: 9g
Calories: 44
Fat: 2g
Price per 100g: 76p (based on £5 cost of tub)

EAT three minis from a Heroes tin and you will outdo a full-size bar for calories. A standard bar has 114 calories, 3.9g fat, while three minis comes in at 132 calories and 6g fat.

Twirl

Full size: 43g
Calories: 230
Fat: 13.2g
Price per 100g: £1.51 (based on 60p cost of bar)

Mini: 7g
Calories: 34
Fat: 1.6g
Price per 100g: 76p (based on £5 cost of tub)

IF you snack on seven Heroes mini Twirls you will get past the 230 calories in a full-size bar. Your feast would add up to 238 calories and 11.2g fat.

Snickers

Full size: 48g
Calories: 245
Fat: 13g
Price per 100g: £1.25 (based on 60p cost of bar)

Mini: 11g
Calories: 54
Fat: 2.7g
Price per 100g: 73.5p (based on £5 box)

FOUR and a half Celebrations mini Snickers – totalling 243 calories and 12.1g fat – is all it takes to compare with the 245-calorie full-size chocolate bar.

Bounty

Full size: 57g
Calories: 278
Fat: 14.6g
Price per 100g: £1.05 (based on 60p cost of bar)

Mini: 10g
Calories: 49
Fat: 2.4g
Price per 100g: 73.5p (based on £5 box)

WOLF five and a half Celebrations mini Bounty chocs for a coconut hit – 270 calories and 13.2g fat – and you are near to the 278 calories in a full-size bar.

Maltesers

Standard bag: 37g
Calories: 186
Fat: 9.1g
Price per 100g: £1.62 (based on 60p cost)

Mini: 9g
Calories: 44 Fat: 2.2g
Price per 100g: 73.5p (based on £5 box)

FOUR and a bit mini Maltesers surely can’t be too bad? But that is 200 calories and 9.8g fat, more than the calories you would find in a standard bag.

Lindt Santa

Full size: 125g
Calories: 550
Fat: 33g
Price per 100g: £2.40 (based on £2.99 for 125g Santa)

Mini: 10g
Calories: 57
Fat: 3.7g
Price per 100g: £5.58 (based on pack cost of £2.79)

SAY goodbye to almost nine and a half baby Saint Nicks – totalling 541 calories, 35.1g fat, and it will be the equivalent of the number of calories in the full-size fella.

Lindt Teddy

Full size: 100g
Calories: 550
Fat: 33g
Price per 100g: £2.99

Mini: 10g
Calories: 57
Fat: 3.7g
Price per 100g: £5.82 (based on £3.49 for 60g pack)

IT takes nine and a half little Lindt teddy bears – 542 calories, 35.1g fat – to match the 100g version. The full size bear packs 550 calories and 33g fat.

Chocolate Orange

Full size: 157g
Calories: 816
Fat: 44g
Price per 100g: 63.7p (based on Orange’s £1 price)

Mini: 8g
Calories: 42
Fat: 2.2g
Price per 100g: £1.33 (based on £4 box price)

WORK your way through a gut-busting 19.5 minis – 819 calories, 42.9g fat – and you will match the calorie damage delivered by one standard-size Chocolate Orange.
