Who is Beto Perez:

Beto Perez is a famous Entrepreneur. He was born on March 15, 1970 and his birthplace is Cali, Colombia. Beto is also well known as, Dancer and entrepreneur who is known for having created the Zumba fitness program during the 1990s. In 2006, he established Zumba Fitness LLC, which sells Zumba fitness products and videos.

Beto is originated from Colombia. His Zumba program has garnered celebrity fans, including Jennifer Lopez and Wyclef Jean.
