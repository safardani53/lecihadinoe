Teen Mom star Jo Rivera's wife Vee shows off curves as she poses in a swimsuit for new video

VEE Rivera flaunted her fit figure in a one piece swimsuit on Tuesday as she promoted her new product that she called a "dream come true."

The Teen Mom 2 star has been teasing the launch of her new venture of late by showing skin in skimpy beachwear.

Vee, 29, took to Instagram on Tuesday to promote her "exciting launch" as she posed for a poolside photoshoot.

The reality star looked stunning in a white one piece featuring green palm fronds and gold chain straps as she lounged with a drink in her hand.

The TV personality tried on two different pairs of sunglasses before sipping her canned beverage, removing her white coverup, and heading to the pool for a dip.

Vee laughed and played with pool floats as she poured her drinks into the water.

To close out the clip, the mother of one turned to the camera and winked in slow motion.

The MTV star captioned her video: "Tomorrow is the big reveal day on something I’ve been working on for quite some time!

"I have always said for years this would be a dream to bring to life & I’m so beyond grateful & happy that I am finally making it come true," she shared.

"Thank you all for always keepin it real with me as I always try and do the same with you.

"Sharing things I love and that we all have in common makes me feel like we have a dope community of some amazing ass women which is all I ever wanted to create! This ones for you!" she gushed, adding: "Stay tuned for tomorrow, it’s going to be SO LIT!"

Last week, Vee nearly slipped out of a leopard print bikini as she shared more content from her promo shoot.

While posing in a deep-v cut one piece swimsuit, the New Jersey native posed in the sunshine as she "prepped" for "hot girl summer."

"Lots of prep going on over here for Hot Girl Summer ‘21," she wrote while showing off lots of skin to her almost 500,000 followers.

"I’ve been working on an exciting new launch that drops next week! It’s the perfect addition to your Hot Girl Summer Activities. I can’t wait to share with y’all what I’ve been working on!"

Vee also changed up her look for a few photos in a bright pink snakeskin bikini set, as she flaunted her toned tummy.

"Confidence, beauty & bikinis have no size or age limit. Rock that s**t babes, you are ABSOLUTELY beautiful," she told her fans.

The Teen Mom star's summer photoshoot follows the reveal of her stunning new swimming pool that her husband Jo Rivera decorated with lights.

Vee filmed her new home addition on her Instagram stories recently, writing: "Hubby put some lights up for me."
