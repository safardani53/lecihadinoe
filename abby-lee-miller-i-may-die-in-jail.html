Abby Lee Miller: I May Die in Jail!

We will likely never see Abby Lee Miller on Dance Moms ever again.

The bully of a host quit this Lifetime reality series in March, accusing producers of not showing her enough support and making life extremely difficult for her over the years.

In an upcoming interview, however, Abby Lee Miller confesses to an even bigger fear than never being seen on television again:

Never being seen on the planet Earth ever again.

“I made lots of mistakes,” Miller tells Jedediah Bila on a special titled Dance Moms: Abby Tells All that will air on July 25, adding:

“I probably won’t survive.”

ABC aired a promo for this ridiculous special on Tuesday and it features Miller crying her eyes out, playing the victim and reciting her resume in light of her much-deserved jail sentence.

(Last year, Miller pleaded guilty to bankruptcy fraud, admitting that she lied to the government about the amount of money she was making for promotional appearances and via other avenues.)

"I panicked,” she says in the following sneak peek. “I gave kids a new life. No one can take that away from me. No one.”

Ever since pleading guilty and then receiving a prison sentence of 366 days, Miller has been on a self-serving promotional tour, attempting to garner sympathy and taking little responsibility for the crimes she committed.

In October 2015, the dance instructor was charged with trying to hide $775,000 of income during Chapter 11 bankruptcy proceedings.

She was even accused of divvying $120,000 and having her friends carry the cash in plastic bags in their luggage in August 2014, which she promised to forfeit in January.

She reported to prison last week.

“I’m afraid of being physically abused or raped,” Miller told People Magazine in April. “I have to stay busy. If I thought about it every day, I’d just sit around and cry."

First off, you’re going to a minimum security prison, Abby. Relax a little bit.

Secondly, you’re going because you’re a liar and you broke the law. There’s only so much sympathy we can garner for you here.

Dance Moms: Abby Tells All airs July 25 and Dance Moms premieres August 1, both at 9/8c on Lifetime.
