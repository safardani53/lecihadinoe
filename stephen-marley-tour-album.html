How can I get tickets to Stephen Marley's tour?

REGGAE singer-songwriter Stephen Marley is set to embark on a tour from September to October 2023.

Here's a quick lowdown on how you can score tickets.

Who is Stephen Marley?

Born on April 20, 1972, in Wilmington, Delaware, Stephen Marley is a Jamaican-American singer-songwriter.

He is the son of legendary reggae star Bob Marley and songwriter Rita Marley.

Stephen started his musical career in 1979 when he became part of the child band Ziggy Marley and the Melody Makers, along with his siblings Ziggy, Sharon, and Cedella.

He also worked as a producer on the side, having collaborated with artists like Erykah Badu, Lauryn Hill, Busta Rhymes, Steven Tyler, and Joe Perry.

In the 2000s, he finally launched his solo career and released his debut album Mind Control in 2007.

Many of his albums earned him Grammy awards, primarily Best Reggae Album trophies.

He currently has eight Grammys to date.

How can I get tickets to Stephen Marley?

Stephen Marley is going on tour from September 2 to October 22, 2023, to promote his Old Soul album.

When is Stephen Marley releasing his album?

Stephen's newest album Old Tour drops on September 15, 2023.

It marks the award-winning artist's first full-length album since 2016.

It features a bevy of special guests, including Eric Clapton, Bob Weir, Jack Johnson, brothers Ziggy Marley and Damian Marley, Buju Banton, and Slightly Stoopid.

Per U Discover Music, Stephen recorded the album during the COVID-19 lockdown in a makeshift studio he set up on a remote farm in Florida.

He describes the sound of his album as "irie vibration."

“You have to be true to yourself,” he said of his recording process.
