Fame | Terry Stokes net worth and salary income estimation Jan, 2024

For over fifty years, Terry Stokes practiced hypnosis as a therapist, lecturer, teacher, and stage performer. During this time, he successfully assisted thousands of people in their quest for self-improvement in such areas as health, weight, sports, education, and success. Terry still enjoys presenting his hypnosis show for entertainment, which he does for over 1 million people each year throughout North America. Terry was a headliner on the Las Vegas Strip at The Stardust and The Tropicana and Planet Hollywood. Terry has authored 3 books as well as 27 audio self-help programs utilizing hypnosis for change. At 70 years old, an age at which most would consider retiring, Terry launched a new career as an actor. After a few local commercials, Terry began getting a few offers for films. His first attempt was a student film called "Lord As My Witness" where Terry played the feature role as, what else, God. From that, he was cast as King Solomon in the film "Voices Beyond the Veil". Terry was also featured in the award-winning short film "Sing No Sad Songs" and will also appear in the new production of "Soul Wars".
