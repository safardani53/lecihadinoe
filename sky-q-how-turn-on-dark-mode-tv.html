I found a secret button on my Sky TV box that everyone should activate today

DARK mode is all the rage and it turns out you can even do it on your Sky box too.

Dark mode is a much-loved feature on all sorts of apps and gadgets now, such as WhatsApp and iPhones.

People love it because it's less strain on your eyes - and even saves battery.

So it seems only natural that people would want it on their Sky Q box too.

While it's not strictly "dark mode" by name, a TikToker has revealed a way that does something quite similar.

When you turn it on, the blue interface is switched for a black one.

"Looks so much better," TikToker James G told followers.

How to find dark mode on Sky Q

There isn't a "dark mode" on Sky Q, at least not by name, but users have figured out do it anyway.

It's officially known as "high contrast".

To activate it, just press Home on the remote.

Find Settings, then go to Accessibility.

Click on High contrast and switch it on.

Your Sky Q should instantly change to a black background.
