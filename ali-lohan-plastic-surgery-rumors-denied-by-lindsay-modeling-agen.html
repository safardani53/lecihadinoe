Ali Lohan Plastic Surgery Rumors Denied By Lindsay, Modeling Agency

Speculation swirling about Ali Lohan getting plastic surgery managed to do the impossible – take the spotlight off that train wreck Lindsay for a day or so.

In a photo taken Tuesday, the 17-year-old aspiring model emerged in Beverly Hills looking drastically different: with fuller lips and a more angular face.

So what happened to her face? According to her modeling agency … age.

Ali Lohan this summer (left) and a few years back.

“Contrary to recent reports, I can confirm that Aliana Lohan has not had any surgery,” said Alexis Borges, agency director at Next Model Management.

“As a young girl who is growing up, it’s natural for her facial features to change slightly, and we see this with many of the younger models we represent.”

“Ali Lohan is a beautiful 17-year-old girl who is growing into her face and body. We encourage models to maintain a healthy lifestyle and body shape.”

Lindsay Lohan also weighed in on the speculation, telling E! her sibling “has never” had surgery. Still, not everyone is convinced. Just look at these pics!
