Who is Rojean Kar? | The US Sun

INSTAGRAMMER, Rojean Kar, has reportedly been the center of Travis-Kylie drama since as far back as 2018.

On October 22, 2022, the model took to social media to stir-up cheating allegations - which Travis Scott has denied.

Who is Rojean Kar?

Rojean Kar is an Instagram model with over 300k followers.

The 30-year-old goes by the name @Yungsweetro on social media, where she often posts steamy, revealing pictures of herself.

Rojean has been linked to rapper, Travis Scott, since as far back as 2013 - way before he started dating fashion mogul, Kylie Jenner.

She was also rumored to be the mistress behind Travis' brief split from Kylie in 2019, however, Rojean referred to the news as “a false narrative," at the time.

In October 2019, the model took to her then-private Instagram account and set the record straight.

"None of these rumours are true, it's just the internet creating a false narrative," she wrote in an Instagram Story obtained by E! News.

"Please stop spreading lies & leave him, her & I alone because it's affecting real lives. Thank you."

What has she said about Travis Scott?

Late last week, Rojean posted a video to her Instagram Story that showed her behind the camera on the set of one of Travis' productions.

The video - in which Travis could be spotted in the background - had “I’m directing obvi 😉” written on it.

Cheating speculation grew amongst fans after the rapper also posted his own pictures from, what looked like, the same set.

However, Travis instantly shut down rumors via his Instagram story on October 22, 2022.

"It's a lot of weird s**t going on," he wrote on Saturday night, denying claims.

"An uninvited person was sneaking photos on, what was supposed to be, a closed set while I was directing a video. I'm saying this for the last time. I don't know this person. I've never been with this person. So please stop with the continuous cyber games and the fictional storytelling."

Rojean also wasted no time telling social media her side of the story.

“What we’re not gonna do is we’re not gonna lie on me. … I pretended I didn’t know you, went along with whatever f–king narrative … no matter how much bulls**t I got from it,” she on an Instagram story video.

“But to say you don’t know me and you’ve never been with me when you’ve definitely been with me, when everybody’s seen you with me, when I have pictures and videos of you with me? Come on. Come on, sir.”

She continued by insisting that she was “was invited to that video shoot,” and that she has “never taken a sneak picture in [her] f**king life.”

Rojean even went as far as to admit that posting the video on set was absolutely “messy."

The model further alleged that she and Scott hung out this past Valentine’s Day and added: “You cheat on that bitch every single f–king night. The whole f–king city sees it! Don’t do this.”

In response, Travis reposted a message from @courtneyluxe, who claimed she’s “been working with travis for 8 years” and “was on set all day as he was directing a video for another artist.”

The woman claimed Kar “was not there with him” and called Rojean “delusional.”

“He does not fck with her in any way. Nothing else to see here 😴😴,” the woman concluded.

How long has Travis been dating Kylie Jenner?

Kylie and Travis have been dating for over five years.

They were first linked in April 2017 and have worked their way through a brief split in October 2019.

The pair were first spotted holding hands together at Coachella in 2017 and welcomed their first child together, Stormi, in February 2018.
