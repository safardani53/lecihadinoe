Who is Ryan Taylor:

Ryan Taylor is a famous YouTube Star. He was born on March 18, 1993 and his birthplace is England. Ryan is also well known as, Professional BMX rider and social media phenomenon who features his world travels and BMX career on his Ryan Taylor BMX YouTube where he has amassed more than 1.8 million subscribers and over 62 million views. He co-founded the company Industry BMX.

Ryan is originated from England. He has been featured in videos with fellow BMX freestyler Harry Main.
