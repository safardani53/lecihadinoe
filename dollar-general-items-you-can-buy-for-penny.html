Three Dollar General items you can buy for one cent - but you have to move fast to get the deals

THREE items have gone on sale as part of the occasional penny list sale from Dollar General.

Starting on February 14, a TikTok creator who posts dozens of couponing hacks has shared when and how to get penny-priced items from Dollar General.

It can be hard to find when the items will go on sale and which items are discounted as the penny list sale is not official.

It's self-proclaimed by extreme couponers who get intel on when the items will be priced at a penny.

Dollar General will sporadically mark items down to one cent to help make discontinued or about-to-expire products easy to identify for employees so they know to remove them from the sale floor, according to The Krazy Coupon Lady.

Usually, the penny-priced items have already been on sale for around 90 percent off for some time also.

People with the knowledge try to snag the one-cent items from the store before employees remove them.

The U.S. Sun has reached out to Dollar General for comment on how many times a year stores put items priced for one cent.

TikTok creator (@madcouponingwithmads) broke it down and made it simple in her video on how to participate during the next penny list sale.

"The Dollar General penny list has been posted and there are a ton of items that are going down to 50 percent off this week," she explained.

The next penny list sale will continue until supplies last.

PENNY PRICED ITEMS

The three items that will only cost one cent this round include:

  • Santa mostaccioli rigatoni noodle
  • Hostess Bouncers
  • Blueberry muffin flavored KitKats

You can price-check items to verify the pricing by downloading the Dollar General app and updating it.

There is a price scanner on the upper right-hand side to scan the barcode.

OTHER DISCOUNTS

Along with the penny list sale, Dollar General will also put major discounts on its products around the same time to help clear out inventory.

The items that have been discounted as of February 14 include:

  • Blue Star Toys will be 50 percent off
  • Fall/Winter apparel, shoes, and accessories with a “blue dot” will be 50 percent off
  • Holiday plastics will be 50 percent off

VARIOUS DOLLAR GENERAL TIPS

There are some other ways to implement shopping hacks on your next visit such as: getting paid to shop, using store coupons, finding exclusive store coupons, and shopping on Friday or Saturday for the best deals.

Along with double checking the pricing at the cash register, looking for Dollar General “dots” indicating major discounted products, and shopping at the Dollar General market for food is another great way to save, per True Money Saver.
