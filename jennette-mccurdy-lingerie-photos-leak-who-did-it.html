Jennette McCurdy Lingerie Photos Leak: Who Did It?!?

Jennette McCurdy, 21, is best known for her role as Sam on Nickelodeon’s iCarly.

But the Web is now abuzz over a photos of her that provide definite eye candy.

A trio of images have hit the Internet showing McCurdy in her bra and underwear. They aren’t especially X-rated or dirty… but the timing of their release has prompted one major question:

Might Andre Drummond be the culprit behind their leak?

Over the weekend, McCurdy appeared on a podcast and laughed at her ex-boyfriend, saying the Detroit Pistons center is a bad kisser and scoffing at how clueless he is.

About the opposite sex, that is. Not basketball.

Andre Drummond and Jennette McCurdy dated last fall after the young NBA player continually gushed over the young actress on Twitter.

In response to McCurdy slamming his lip-locking prowess and going off in lengthy, negative detail about him overall, Drummond told TMZ:

“There’s a lot of false info in there, but I’ll be the mature person about the situation and let it die out.”

Which he very well may have done. Or he may have gone in the opposite direction and anonymously leaked three racy Jennette McCurdy pictures online. 

UPDATE: Drummond has denied playing a role in this leak, despite implications seemingly made by McCurdy, who tweeted about the pics, writing:

“To anyone disappointed: I sent those pics to 1 person. You can connect the dots. Shocked someone would stoop so low. I just speak w/ candor.”

Drummond, though, tells TMZ, “I did NOT leak any photos of JM” and adds, “I want nothing to do with her or the situation. I’m just worried about ball.”
