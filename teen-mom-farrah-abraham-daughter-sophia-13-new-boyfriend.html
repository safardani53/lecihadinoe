Teen Mom fans shocked as Farrah Abraham's daughter Sophia, 13, debuts new boyfriend on TikTok

FARRAH Abraham's teenage daughter appears to have a new boyfriend.

And Teen Mom fans are all about it - sending the 13-year-old much praise and support.

On Monday, Sophia Abraham shared a cute video to TikTok of her and her unnamed beau making a Build-a-Bear.

The clip only provided a quick, shaky glance at his face, but the boy's giant red hair fell to his shoulder.

He appears a little taller than Sophia, and looks to be around the same age.

The young man also seemed to echo Sophia's love for the dark, wearing an all-black shirt, but matching it with khaki pants.

Sophia wore a black shirt and black pants. She also sported some heavy black platform shoes that gave her some lift.

She smiled and laughed throughout the trip, as the two sweetly added a stuffed heart to their new pet.

Keeping with the black theme, the two settled on stuffing a "Toothless" dragon from the movie How to Train Your Dragon.

"He got me a build a bear plushie," Sophia captioned the clip, adding three heart emojis at the end.

FARRAH'S FAILURE

Fans have not been shy about slamming Farrah's parenting skills, especially after the 31-year-old went partying with Sophia at an 18+ club.

The pair danced at The Empire Control Room, a club in Texas, on July 18.

The former Teen Mom OG star posted videos to her Instagram and TikTok Stories of her listening to music as singers performed at the club.

Rapper Lil Darkie and a few others took the stage with flashing lights and an enthusiastic crowd.

Her next few Stories showed everyone holding up their phones and enjoying the music.

In two of the Stories, Farrah moved the camera over to show Sophia, standing next to her.

The young girl rocked out to the music and had a huge smile on her face.
