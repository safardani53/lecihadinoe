Kylie Jenner Wears Blue Contacts, Rocks Her Curves in Mesh Dress: Pics

For fun

A photo posted by Kylizzle (@kyliejenner) on Apr 15, 2015 at 5:54pm PDT

She’s got the blues!

Kylie Jenner‘s plump pout may be the star of her Instagram page, but it got pushed aside on Wednesday, April 15, when a different facial feature came into focus.

“For fun,” the 17-year-old social media darling captioned the photo, which featured her brown eyes disguised by ocean-hued contact lenses. Of course, her pucker, lined in rose pink, was featured too, alongside her lush lashes, bold brows, and just a peek at her mesh ensemble.

That same day, Kim Kardashian‘s half-sister stepped out (to Beverly Hills’ Nail Bar and Beauty Lounge), giving fans the opportunity to ogle her complete dress: a long-sleeved LBD with peekaboo panels and a very short hemline, teamed with a feathered bag and lace-up booties.

Look closely, and you’ll see another Kim-and-Kylie-look-alike moment: The see-through number was a near-match to a mesh-topped jumpsuit Kardashian, 34, wore to the Brit Awards in London this past February.
