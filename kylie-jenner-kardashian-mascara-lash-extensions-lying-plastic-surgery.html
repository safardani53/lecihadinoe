Kylie Jenner accused of 'lying' after she claims she does not have lash extensions in new photo prom

KYLIE Jenner has taken to Instagram to post a new advertisement for her new volume mascara but may have been caught in a lie.

A fan accused Kylie, 25, of lying about having eyelash extensions in the dramatic closeup photos of the Kardashian star's thick lashes.

Kylie advertised her new mascara with a dramatically lit photo of her eye.

The lighting fell so her eyes were in the light and the top and bottom of her face were in the shadows.

Kylie's lashes appeared extra-long, compared to times when she has claimed she's "all-natural" in photos.

One fan asked Kylie: "Do you have lash extensions on in this picture?"

"I don't!" the star replied.

However, fans were not convinced as one said in the thread: "Girl, don't you lie like that."

A second person remarked: "You sure?"

Yet a third shared: "I know extensions when I see them, Kylie."

Kylie has faced even more accusations of wearing lash extensions for her mascara advertisements.

"Like, I’m so sick of influencers getting Botox and then selling skincare."

TOO FAR!

The Hulu star has also been under speculation from fans that she may have gone too far with her alleged plastic surgery.

After an unedited picture of the mother-of-two at the Vanity Fair Oscars afterparty was released, some fans couldn't help but point out how different she looked from her teenage years and speculated that she has had surgery.

One shared a professional photo of her face from the event online and wrote: "I feel so sad for her that she permanently ruined her face."

Another claimed she had surgery to make herself look more wide-eyed.

The fan said: "I’m amazed she can close [her eyes] at this point. Their plastic surgeon needs to lose his license."

A third claimed Kylie - who admitted to getting lip fillers as a teenager - went under the knife too young.
