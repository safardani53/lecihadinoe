Antiques Roadshow viewers distracted by Fiona Bruces appearance - but can you spot why?

FIONA Bruce’s appearance on Sunday night's episode of Antiques Roadshow completely distracted BBC One viewers from the rest of the episode.

BBC host Fiona Bruce returned to Bishop’s Palace in Wells, Somerset in yesterday’s episode of Antiques Roadshow, but her bold outfit choice distracted viewers from the Fabergé jewellery on show. 

While the 58-year-old TV presenter was busy studying the three Fabergé jewellery pieces on display so she could rank them in order of “Basic, Better, Best,” viewers were busy looking at her outfit. 

She wore a flattering wide-leg denim jumpsuit which had mid-length sleeves and was cinched at the waist.  

Her stunning appearance got viewers talking online. 

One viewer said: “Fiona looks amazing tonight.”

“Fiona doing the double denim #Antiques Roadshow,” a second viewer praised. 

A third commented: “I want Fiona Bruce’s jumpsuit #Antiques Roadshow.”

Viewers were impressed with Fiona’s ability to pull off the double-denim style jumpsuit, which she paired with white trainers. 

One viewer dubbed it “Fiona Bruce’s Sunday night anti-fear denim jumpsuit of hope.”

While viewers were admiring Fiona’s outfit, she went on to ask audience members which piece of Fabergé jewellery they thought was the most expensive. 

However, they all gave her conflicting opinions.
