Who is Michael Angelo Batio:

Michael Angelo Batio is a famous Guitarist. He was born on February 23, 1956 and his birthplace is Chicago, IL. Michael is also well known as, Renowned guitarist who rose to fame as a member of the metal band Nitro in the 1980s. He gained notoriety for his showmanship, wielding double and quad guitars, playing sweeping arpeggios, and using his trademark “over-under” playing technique. He was named by Guitar One Magazine as the No. 1 Shredder of All Time in 2003.

Michael is originated from United States. He has given guitar lessons to artists like Rage Against the Machine’s Tom Morello and Creed’s Mark Tremonti.
