Dan Harmon's Married Life with Ex-Wife Erin McGathy

Dan Harmon who is titled a comedy genius was previously married to a talented podcast host Erin McGathy. The couple tied the knot in November 2014 at Los Angeles Natural History Museum. Before getting married, they dated for a pretty long time.

Well, their marital relationship soon turned sour as time passed. Nearly after a year of the couple's marriage, disheartening news came that the couple is no more together. McGathy took to Twitter to announce her divorce from Dan. Check it out:

People may say getting a divorce is an extreme measure for rebooting a podcast, but I'd ask if they heard my interview w/ Rachael Leigh Cook

— Erin McGathy (@ErinMcGathy) October 11, 2015

It was again on October 11, 2015, when Erin made a tweet in which she talked about her divorce with Dan and asked people to respect their privacy. Have a look:

Sad to announce that @danharmon and I are divorcing. We ask that you respect our privacy as we discuss it openly on our respective podcasts

— Erin McGathy (@ErinMcGathy) October 11, 2015
