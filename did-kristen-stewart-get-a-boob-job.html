But with this rumor making the rounds, it’s our job to ask and report on our source:

“Her breasts have gone from what looked like a large A cup to a large B cup,” says the New York City-physician, Dr. Anthony Youn out of Michigan adds:

“While this might be the result of a new, state-of-the-art bra, it’s most likely the result of a breast augmentation.”
