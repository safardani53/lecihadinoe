Kim Kardashian slammed for 'cruel' treatment of house staff after she leaves a 'total mess' in bathr

KIM Kardashian has been slammed online after leaving her house a mess.

The Hulu star is continuing to prepare her home for Christmas - but many fans are concerned for her housekeepers.

In a new post on her Instagram Stories, Kim, 43, showed off a holiday-themed mess that took over her bathroom inside the Kardashian's $60million mansion.

In the first clip, Kim showed off the gorgeous lit-up trees outside the room and then panned her phone over the bathtub made out of stone.

Instead of water, the tub was filled with chocolate.

Meanwhile, festive-themed candies including Hershey Kisses, marshmallows, and sprinkles were scattered all over the table next to the tub.
