Bridget Marquardt: Wedding Planner Extraordinnaire!

They may not be roommates anymore, but Bridget Marquardt and Kendra Wilkinson are still BFFs.

Need proof? Bridget told Us Weekly that she’s in charge of the bachelorette party for her close pal and bride-to-be.

“I am in charge of the bachelorette party [and the bridal] shower,” Marquardt said. “There are other girls, too… Some playmates and her best friend from San Diego. I think there are five of us all together. It is not too big of a wedding.”

Wilkinson, who is marrying Philadelphia Eagles’ Hank Baskett on June 27 at the  Playboy Mansion, is filming all the wedding activities for her new E! reality show, Kendra.

“They have asked me to collaborate with them,” Marquardt said. “They are looking into a couple different venues right now. They have to pick the venue because of filming and permits and all that kind of stuff. The film crew wants to come with me to scout a few different locations this week.”
