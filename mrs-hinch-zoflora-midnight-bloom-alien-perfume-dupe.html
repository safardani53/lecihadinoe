AS self-confessed Zoflora addicts, we're forever wishing that the brand will one day release their beautiful range of scents in perfume form.

But while this sadly may still be a far off dream, Mrs Hinch fans are going wild for the brand's new Midnight Bloom disinfectant which they claim smells just like Thierry Mugler's iconic Alien perfume.

  • Zoflora Disinfectant in Midnight Blooms, £1.50 from Thorton & Ross Direct - buy now

Earlier this month, Zoflora launched their new limited edition disinfectant in Asda, Tesco, Sainsbury’s and Morrisons.

Posting on the Facebook group Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK, one delighted shopper wrote: "These smell just like Alien perfume and it smells amazing!!"

According to the product description, the new Zoflora scent has floral rose top notes and amber undertones.

In comparison, Thierry Mugler's Alien fragrance has a cult following thanks to its rich amber scent with woody and jasmine aromas.

  • Thierry Mugler Alien Perfume, £78 for 60ml from The Perfume Shop - buy now

But while a bottle of Alien perfume will set you back almost £80, the new Zoflora disinfectant costs only £1.50 - and it can be used on everything from descaling shower heads to scenting rooms.

And to be honest, we care more about our homes smelling lovely than having a new perfume anyway...!

Needless to say, others shoppers are now desperate to get their hands on the new scent after the comparison was made - with one commenting: "Need this ASAP!"

As with all of their products, Zoflora's latest addition to the range kills 99.9 per cent of bacteria and has become wildly popular since Mrs Hinch shot to fame in 2018.

The star uses the disinfectant on everything from her kitchen sink to mopping her floors.
