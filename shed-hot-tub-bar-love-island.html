We turned our shed into hot tub bar - people fret TV will steam up but theres nothing better than

A COUPLE decided to give their shed a summer makeover inspired by Love Island and transformed it into a hot tub and bar, complete with a TV to watch their favourite show.

TikTok user @homely_no12 found themselves going viral on social media after sharing a tour of their unconventional shed in their back garden.

Unlike any other shed, the inside is a little haven for spending nights in and has a huge hot tub with a flat screen TV sat on the side next to it.

Showing off the cool hangout space in their video - which has been watched over 6.2million times, the couple teased: "Everybody has that one thing in their house that everybody thinks is so cool.

"Could be a hidden cabinet, a little movie room, a gadget... but ours is this."

The hot tub setup also includes a fully stocked bar and snacks to tuck into while indulging in the latest Love Island episode, as well as candles and cool prints on the wall.

Not surprisingly, the pair have been inundated with questions over how exactly it works having a hot tub inside their shed, with some people concerned it could be very "hot" and "muggy" and the the TV could steam up.

Insisting it's not, the shed owners revealed in a follow-up video: "It's not confined or hot or muggy as you can see the whole front opens up."

When in use, they open the shed's double doors at the front to let air in.

"The roof has huge vents for ventilation and we have a de-humidifier for moisture control," they revealed.

Making sure enough light can get through, they also added a skylight into the roof of shed.

People were all for the transformation with nearly half-a-million liking the post.

Now that's one way to watch Love Island in style...
