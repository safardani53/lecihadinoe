Kanye West and Sessilee Lopez

Sessilee Lopez may be a burgeoning model for Victoria’s Secret, but the word is out about her relationship with Kanye West.

According to sources at The Sun, Lopez – who appeared in West’s music video for “Flashing Lights” – has been spending a lot of intimate time with the rapper.

“Kanye was instantly smitten with Sessilee and it’s not hard to see why,” said the source. “But it’s not just her looks that attracted Kanye; they get along really well and have a definite connection.”

The anonymous insider added:

“They have spent a lot of time together recently as Kanye has invited her along to numerous parties. They’ve also spent some down time together.”

Kanye West has reportedly moved on from his split with ex-fiancee Alexis Phifer. He’s made the upgrade to Sessilee Lopez.
