Angela Simmons Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Bio:
Angela Simmons (born September 18, 1987 in Queens, New York) is a reality show personality and designer. She starred on the reality show Run’s House and was the editor for a mini magazine called Angela’s Rundown until 2012. She is featured in Bow Wow‘s music video for “Shortie Like Mine”, and launched a shoe collection with her sister Vanessa Simmons in 2007 called Pastry Footwear. She currently stars on and executive produces the reality show Growing Up Hip Hop.

Best Known For:
Angela Simmons is best known for being a reality show star and the daughter of Rev Run of Run-D.M.C.

Personal Life:

Angela attended the Fashion Institute of Technology but left school to become a businesswoman. She has dated the rapper Bow Wow. In April 2016, Angela announced that she was engaged via Instagram, but the identity of her fiance remains a mystery.
