Kim Kardashian mocked for 'tacky' wrapping paper on Christmas presents and suspect she 'faked' prepa

KIM Kardashian has been mocked for how she wrapped her Christmas presents on her sister Kourtney's Poosh gift feature.

The Skims founder chose mirrored silver wrapping to match her monochromatic 2022 palette, but fans are not enthusiastic about her seasonal aesthetic choice.

Kourtney, 43, published a Poosh article about how all of the Kardashians excluding Kylie, 25, wrapped their gifts this year.

While Kris, 67, Kendall, 27, and Khloe, 38, kept a traditional approach to their wrapping with Santa Claus and snowmen prints, Kourtney chose a sustainable wrapping with biodegradable twine.

However, Kim, 42, went out-of-this world with her wrapping scheme.

She wrapped all of her presents in a silver, mirrored, futuristic film and fans are skeptical of the strong choice.

FESTIVE FAMILY

Kim even kept her monochromatic theme going inside her $60M Calabasas mansion for her Christmas decorations.

In her living room, the bushy pine tree is minimalist and decorated with only white fairy lights.

The theme continued outside where Kim transformed the trees in her front yard to create her own winter wonderland.

All of the trees were lined with more white fairy lights.

Kim abandoned the monochrome theme inside of her children's rooms.

Each of her four kids has their own personalized Christmas trees and Kim's receiving backlash over how she decorated one covered in $100 bills.

LAVISH LIFE

Kim also hires a private pianist for the entire month of December to wake her kids up with Christmas music.

However, fans have been appalled by Kim's tradition and feel the expensive activity is out of touch.

Fans took to an online forum to express their criticism.

One fan wrote: "Isn't it so out of touch to have a pianist in your home for Christmas days? Kim uses every type of paid help at her house."
