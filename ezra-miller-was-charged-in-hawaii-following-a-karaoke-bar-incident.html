Ezra Miller Was Charged In Hawaii Following A Karaoke Bar Incident

On March 28, Ezra Miller was apprehended by police in Hilo, Hawaii. After an altercation at a karaoke club, the 29-year-old was charged with disorderly behavior and harassment. Following the payment of a $500 bond, the actor was freed from detention. Representatives for The Flash and Warner Bros. have yet to comment on the matter.

The reason behind Ezra Miller’s arrest

According to the Hawaii County Police Department, officers from South Hilo responded to a report of a rowdy bar patron around 11:30 p.m. on March 27. Miller allegedly grew belligerent as other guests began singing karaoke and shouted obscenities. The Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them star then seized the microphone from a 23-year-old lady singing karaoke and rushed at a 32-year-old guy playing darts. Miller’s arrest comes at an inconvenient moment for Warner Bros. since the actor is due to participate in the studio’s forthcoming film, Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore. The film is set to be released on April 15, 2022.

Ezra Miller’s legal history

Ezra Miller was a passenger in a car that was pulled over in 2011 for a broken brake light. They were discovered to be in possession of 20 grams of marijuana by the police. Miller was arrested and charged with narcotics possession. The accusations, however, were eventually withdrawn by a court. The Justice League actor was fined $600 for two counts of disruptive behavior. In April 2020, a video of Miller choking a lady and tossing her to the ground became viral on Twitter. According to Variety, the event occurred in Reykjavik at Priki Kaffihs, a pub frequented by the actor. Miller was recognized in the video by a bartender. After the altercation, the personnel escorted them from the premises.

Miller posted an Instagram video in January 2022 in which they seemed to threaten members of the Ku Klux Klan operating in Beulaville, North Carolina. The Southern Poverty Law Center, on the other hand, had no information of any Klan activity in the region.

Future films starring Ezra Miller

Ezra Miller will shortly be featured in two major motion pictures: Fantastic Beasts: The Secrets of Dumbledore and The Flash. The third part of the Fantastic Beasts film series, directed by David Yates, will be released on April 15, 2022. In addition to Eddie Redmayne, Jude Law, Dan Fogler, Alison Sudol, Callum Turner, Jessica Williams, Katherine Waterston, Richard Coyle, and Mads Mikkelsen, the ensemble cast includes Eddie Redmayne, Jude Law, Dan Fogler, Alison Sudol, Callum Turner, Jessica Williams, Katherine Waterston, Richard Coyle, and Mads Mikkelsen in lead roles.

Meanwhile, The Flash, directed by Andy Muschietti, will be released on June 23, 2023. It is the 14th film in the DCEU, and it stars Miller, Ron Livingston, Michael Keaton, Kiersey Clemons, Michael Shannon, Antje Traue, Sasha Calle, and Ben Affleck.
