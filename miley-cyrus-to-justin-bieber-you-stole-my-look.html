Miley Cyrus to Justin Bieber: You Stole My Look!

Are Miley Cyrus and Justin Bieber the same person?

Of course not. That would be ridiculous.

But in an interview with E! News, the female artist did call out The Biebs for trying to be look her.

"I saw him the other day and I had to say, ‘Bitch stole my look!’" Cyrus joked while promoting her new lipstick and gloss for MAC Cosmetics’ Viva Glam.

(All proceeds benefit from the sales of Miley’s new lipstick will benefit the MAC AIDS Fund.)

Miley, of course, went blonde many years ago, while Bieber dyed his hair platinum a couple months ago.

"I literally said it to him and he said, ‘I’m growing my hair out.’ I said, ‘Yeah, me, too.’ It’s not easy with bleach. It’s harder than it looks. He said, ‘How long have you been growing your hair out?’ I said, ‘Since I cut it in 2012.’"

Cyrus explained that she wantss to grow her locks back out. But it’s a challenge.

"Talk to my scalp…I mean, I’m trying," she said. "I keep getting it here [she moves her hand slightly below her hairline] and then I keep bleaching it, then cutting it all off."
