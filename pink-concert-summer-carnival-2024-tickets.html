How can I buy tickets for Pinks 2024 Summer Carnival tour?

POP-rock icon Pink's Summer Carnival Tour has already grossed over $350 million, but the singer's not calling it quits just yet.

Pink is set to extend her 2024 Summer Carnival Tour with new dates across North America, performing her greatest hits along with new tracks from her ninth studio album, Trustfall.

How can I buy tickets for Pink’s 2024 concert tour?

Tickets for the Summer Carnival Tour are already on sale for select dates and locations.

Fans hoping to see Pink perform in the UK, Ireland, or Europe are in luck, as tickets are still available on the singer's website.

They can also be purchased directly via Ticketmaster.

Some third-party sites are also selling tickets, including StubHub, SeatGeek, and Vivid Seats.

Fans should keep in mind that while third-party resellers are a great way to snag last-minute tickets, especially to sold-out venues, ticket prices may be higher or lower depending on demand.

Tickets for newly added dates to the Summer Carnival 2024 Tour will be available for purchase starting on Monday, December 11, 2023.

Tickets will go on sale at 10:00 am local time, meaning a show in LA will have tickets available for purchase at 10:00 am PST, while a show in Chicago will have tickets available at 10:00 am CST.

Early access and presale tickets are available now, but fans need to have Verizon Up or a Citi rewards credit card to access the sale.

Third-party resellers have also already begun selling tickets to the newly added dates ahead of the December 11 rollout.

What can fans expect from the tour?

Pink announced the newly added dates of her Summer Carnival 2024 tour via social media.

She took to Instagram to share the news, posting a reel with the additional stops' dates and locations.

Pink captioned the post: "NORTH AMERICA – we had the BEST TIME with you this year, so we HAD to add more dates in 2024 and bring my friends @sherylcrow, @thescriptofficial, and @kidcutup with me" along with a celebratory confetti emoji.

She added: "Can’t wait to see you there!! Tickets on sale Mon Dec 11th!"

The Summer Carnival 2024 Tour includes several special guests, including The Script, Rag'n'Bone Man, Gayle, and KidCutUp.

Pink's newly added dates, in her tour's second North American leg, will also include performances by the legendary country music star, Sheryl Crow, along with The Script and KidCutUp.

The tour has received positive reviews from critics, who praise the singer's vocals along with the show's extravagance.

Pop Matter's Sachyn Mital described the tour as "less a carnival and more of a circus with the charismatic and candid singer as the ringleader of the unique pop spectacle."

Paul Brannigan from Louder Sound said: "Quite honestly, there's so much going on in this show that you could write a book about it."

In an interview with Good Morning America, Pink herself said that her newest album, Trustfall, will "make listeners want to 'take their clothes off and dance.'"

Casual and diehard fans alike will find something to enjoy in Pink's 2024 Summer Carnival Tour.

The tour, now running through November 2024, proves that summer isn't just a season – it's a state of mind.

What are the Summer Carnival tour dates?

Pink's 2024 tour, Summer Carnival, kicks off in June in the UK.

The singer will make 18 stops in the UK, Ireland, and Europe in June and July.

She'll then move on to the North American leg of her Summer Carnival Tour, which now runs from August to November 2024.

On December 5, 2023, the pop vocalist added 17 brand-new dates in North America.

Pink's complete tour now includes 41 stops worldwide.

The pop-rock icon will perform at the following dates and locations:
