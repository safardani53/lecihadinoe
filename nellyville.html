Nellyville - BET - Watch on Paramount Plus

Cornell Haynes Jr. -- aka Nelly -- is much more than a multiplatinum hip-hop artist and a successful entrepreneur. The docuseries Nellyville -- the title of his 2002 album -- profiles a loving father, friend and mentor. He is raising his kids -- Chanelle and Cornell -- and his deceased sister's kids -- Sydney and Lil Shawn, and he has a blossoming relationship with actress/model Shantel Jackson. Nelly's business ventures include a fashion line and part-ownership of the NBA's Charlotte Hornets.
