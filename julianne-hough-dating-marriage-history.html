Who has Julianne Hough dated?

PROFESSIONAL dancer Julianne Hough has made her return to Dancing With the Stars on season 32.

Fans of the multitalented 35-year-old want to know more about her past marriage and current relationship status.

Who is Julianne Hough?

Julianne Alexandra Hough was born in Orem, Utah, on July 20, 1988.

She is a dancer, actress, and singer best known for being a cast member of Dancing with the Stars.

Hough joined the show as a professional dancer back in 2007 but left after only two years. From 2014 to 2017, she served as a permanent judge on the popular ABC series.

On September 23, 2023, the TV personality returned to Dancing With the Stars for season 32 of the show, but this time, to co-host with Alfonso Ribeiro.

When was Julianne Hough married?

Over the years, Hough has been engaged twice and married once.

She began dating hockey player Brooks Laich in 2013 and by August 2015 the pair were engaged. 

"I'm just feeling so happy and blessed every moment of every day," Hough told People at the time. "My favorite thing about being engaged is just a sense of warmth and just joy."

In July 2017, Hough and Laich tied the knot in Coeur d'Alene, Idaho, however, after three years of marriage the pair announced they were separating.

"We have lovingly and carefully taken the time we have needed to arrive at our decision to separate," Hough and Laich told People in a joint statement in May 2020.

"We share an abundance of love and respect for one another and will continue to lead with our hearts from that place."

Their divorce was finalized in 2022, and the pair still remain family friends.

Who has Julianne Hough dated?

Hough was first engaged to her hometown friend, Zack Wilson, at the early age of 18, however by November 2007, they decided to call it quits.

Since then, the star has dated a number of celebrities, including:

  • Country music star Chuck Wicks (2008 - 2009)
  • Comedian Dane Cook (unconfirmed)
  • TV personality Ryan Seacrest (2010 - 2013)
  • Academy Award-winning director Ari Sandel (unconfirmed)
  • Model Charlie Wilson (unconfirmed)
