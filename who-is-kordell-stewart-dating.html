Kordell Stewart was born on a Monday, October 16, 1972 in New Orleans. His birth name is Kordell Stewart and he is currently 51 years old. People born on October 16 fall under the zodiac sign of Libra. His zodiac animal is Rat.

Kordell Stewart, nicknamed “Slash,” is a former American football quarterback who played eleven years in the National Football League (NFL). Stewart attended the University of Colorado and was the 60th player selected in the 1995 NFL draft. Playing for Colorado in 1994 he completed a Hail Mary pass to beat the University of Michigan 2726, a play which became known as “The Miracle at Michigan.” Among NFL quarterbacks, his 38 rushing touchdowns ranks second all-time, behind Steve Young’s 43. The NFL Network named him #6 on its list of the 10 most versatile players in NFL history.

He set school records at the University of Colorado, including most passing yards and most passing touchdowns.
