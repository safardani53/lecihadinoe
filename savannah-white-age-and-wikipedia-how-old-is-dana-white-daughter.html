How Old Is Dana White Daughter?

Dana White’s daughter, Savannah White, has been the media cracker from a young age- she is currently in her teens.

Even if she may try to stay low-key, as the daughter of the MMA promotion honcho, it won’t be a piece of cake.

Savannah is the youngest of three children of the UFC president and CEO and his wife, Annie White.

Unlike her elder siblings (two brothers), she has not taken a professional route in sports. However, given her interest in gymnastics, hopeful eyes are set on her upcoming career flight.

Savannah White is the only daughter of Dana White and the one with a private life in the White family. So, fans are intrigued to know more about her.

Ever since her early days, her name’s circling the media– for instance, getting an ear bitten off by a dog and getting threats.

While it’s relatively away from camera eyes, the American businessman and his daughter share a great bond. They make occasional yet very impactful appearances, which is one of the influential definitions of the father-daughter duo.

Savannah White Age & Wikipedia: How Old Is Dana White’s Daughter?

Dana’s daughter, Savannah, was born on August 23, 2006, in Pine Island Court, Las Vegas. She is 17 years old as of 2023.

In August 2022, the UFC promoter, who owns a $500 million net worth, threw a birthday bash on his daughter on her 16th birthday, which became quite news then.

As reported, White rented out all of Jewel Nightclub at Arita for the party; estimations were nearly $1 million. He snagged off the same expenses for his second son Aidan’s 16th birthday in 2018.

In 2023, the UFC president wished his daughter a 17th birthday via Instagram story, writing, “Happy 17th birthday, baby @savy_white”.

Moreover, he also addressed that he was short of pictures with his baby girl, stating, “I need to update some new pics on my phone.”

The youngest of three kids, Savannah White’s age gap with her brothers, Dana White III and Aidan White, is almost five and four years, respectively.

Her eldest brother, Dana III, born on June 9, 2001, is 22 years old, while her other brother, Aidan, born on July 18, 2002, is 21.

Unlike her father, she doesn’t have a Wikipedia page.

Dana White Is Protective Yet Supportive Of Daughter Savannah

White, raised a Catholic who now considers him atheist, has an emphasized belief in parental love, especially for his teen daughter, Savannah.

Even if he’s been keeping his family away from the media, sometimes he can’t help but talk about them.

On November 14, 2023, during an interview with Theo Von in the podcast This Past Weekend, Dana talked about his priority for his daughter. He stated he has ‘certain rules for his daughter, which he doesn’t for his sons’.

Clarifying the reason, White said, “As a dad, you have to sort of oversee and protect your daughter more than you did your sons.”

The protective dad made a highlight in November 2022 after he caught the mystery man who allegedly threatened his daughter, who was none other than Brandon Bender, an Italian-American MMA fighter.

In March 2023, during an episode of The Joe Rogan Experience, White recalled a moment when his then-nine-year-old Savannah’s ear was bitten off by their English mastiff dog.

As a result, he missed the UFC 192, where Daniel Cormier and Alexander Gustafsson were fighting for the light heavyweight title.

As reported, Dana called a plastic surgeon in his home instead of a doctor, who fixed his daughter’s ear.

What Does Savannah White Do?

Savannah White is a junior high school attendant; she will go to college in a few years.

On November 3, 2023, Dana shared an Instagram post featuring his daughter practicing gymnastics at UFC Performance Institute. However, during the landing, she lost her balance and was seen limping after getting up.

White tagged his daughter in the video and wrote, “BOOOM! And there goes her knee.” There are no further updates on the injury– hope it’s not severe.

Savannah’s brothers are already on their way to a professional career in sports. The eldest brother, Dana III, opted out of kickboxing and found a love for football.

He is a collegiate football player at the University of San Diego.

On the other hand, Adian Charles White is an amateur boxer who debuted in 2018.
