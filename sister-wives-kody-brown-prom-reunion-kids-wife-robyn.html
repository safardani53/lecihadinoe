SISTER Wives' Kody Brown missed out on his daughters' prom celebration and reunion with son, Hunter, as fans suspect he has been in quarantine with his "favorite" wife, Robyn.

The Brown family patriarch has been visibly absent from the family's celebrations lately.

Kody has seemingly been missing out on spending precious family time with his wives and children amid the coronavirus pandemic.

First, he was absent when Christine Brown set up a photoshoot for their two daughters, Ysabel and Gwendlyn, who were meant to go to prom last weekend.

Instead of returning the dresses or letting them go to waste, the doting mom had the girls glam up and pose as if their magical night had really happened.

Ysabel shared the sweet pictures on her Instagram and only tagged her mom and her two sisters.

Along with the pictures of her in a green dress, she wrote: "Just for kicks and giggles...we drove almost two hours to take pictures in over priced dresses :))"

The teen failed to tag her dad in the photos.

Kody has also been missing out on spending time with Janelle Brown and their kids.

Hunter has reportedly not seen his dad since recently returning home after graduating from the United States Air Force Academy.

None of his family members could attend his graduation ceremony because of the pandemic, however, all but Kody, were quick to receive him with open arms as he returned home.

Janelle posted a photo on Sunday of Hunter and two of her other sons eating together.

She wrote: "First dinner with Hunter since he arrived home for summer break.

"I obviously raised gentlemen with great table manners."

And as for Meri Brown, she's been enjoying some alone time.

On Sunday, she shared a series of videos on her Instagram Stories of what fans think is Coyote Pass- the Brown family's property.

Alongside the sweet videos of her dog exploring the area, she wrote: "Sunday exploring."

These pictures further solidify Reddit's speculations that Kody is hunkering down with Robyn Brown as the coronavirus pandemic sweeps the country.

Fans had previously pointed out the other wives - Christine, Meri and Janelle Brown - have been posting pictures on their social media without him.

One fan wrote: "I think we all know the answer to this ... They would use the excuse that she has all the youngest kids.

"And they're the ones that would need 2 parents there for quarantine to help with the school work and things like that. But we all know it's just so that he can be with her for two weeks straight."

Recently, Meri admitted that she has "hard feelings" after snubbing her husband, Kody, on their failed 30th anniversary.

Neither Meri nor Kody acknowledged their milestone wedding anniversary on social media, further hinting the demise of their relationship.
