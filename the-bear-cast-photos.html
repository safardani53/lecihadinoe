Photos Of Jeremy Allen White &amp; More Hollywood Life

The critically-acclaimed series ‘The Bear’ returned for its highly-anticipated second season on June 22. All 10 episodes dropped at once. The new season picked up where season 1 left off as Carmy, Sydney, Richie, and the rest of the crew worked together to transform The Beef into The Bear.

The transformation was a long process, and they had to strip the restaurant down to its bones. The journey was illuminating for everyone involved, especially Marcus, who traveled to Copenhagen to come up with new desserts for the restaurant.

Series regulars include Jeremy Allen White, Ayo Edebiri, Ebon Moss-Bachrach, Lionel Boyce, Liza Colón-Zayas, Abby Elliot, and Matty Matheson. Edwin Lee Gibson, Oliver Platt, and Molly Gordon appeared in recurring roles. Season 2 also featured a number of amazing guest stars, including Jamie Lee Curtis, Sarah Paulson, Olivia Colman, Will Poulter, and more. See photos of the incredible cast in our gallery now.
