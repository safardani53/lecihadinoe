Jennifer Lopez silences pregnancy rumours with her toned stomach

JENNIFER Lopez has silenced those who claimed she is pregnant by posting a picture of herself with rock hard abs.

Taken with a fan after one of her Las Vegas show’s this week, the star can clearly been seen with a toned tum and no hint of a baby bump.

She also sent the same photo to TMZ to make sure they knew she wasn’t expecting.

It had been claimed that the Jenny From The Block singer was going to have a baby with her long term boyfriend Casper Smart, 29.

But in the picture she is wearing a bandeau crop top which clearly shows her six pack.

The picture was backed up by her manager and old friend Benny Medina, who told TMZ that the rumours were “100% not true”.

Then, talking about the photo she posted, he said: “Does this look like pregnant?”

J-Lo is currently in Vegas where she is performing a year-long residency at Planet Hollywood, which is scheduled to run through to the end of the year.
