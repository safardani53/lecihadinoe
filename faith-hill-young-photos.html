Happy birthday Faith Hill! Celebrate the singer’s special day with the best photos of her through the years!

Faith Hill, born September 21, 1967, is one of the biggest country stars of all time. She moved to Nashville with a dream at 19, and made her debut with 1993’s Take Me as I Am. Faith went from country cutie to mainstream and crossover success with her next two albums, Faith (1998) and Breathe (1999). She earned major radio play with the songs “This Kiss”, “Breathe” and “The Way You Love Me”. Her later singles “Cry”, “Mississippi Girl” and “Like We Never Loved at All” were also hits. Since her start she’s sold 40 million records worldwide and earned 5 Grammys.

Faith is also one-half of a country power couple. She married Tim McGraw in 1996 and they’ve been going strong ever since. Want to see more photos of Faith through the years? Find her from her early Nashville days to full blown superstardom here.
