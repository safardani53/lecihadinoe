How old is Sir Cruse and what's his net worth?

SIR Cruse is a YouTube star with 554,000 subscribers.

He posted a TikTok video to clear up what his age is after Google search results claimed he was 29 years old.

Who is Sir Cruse?

Sir Cruse, real name William Cruse, is a 54 year old YouTuber.

He's known for posting videos on hair styles, particularly ones that involve waves.

Some sources claim he's 29, but he set the record straight in a video.

He said: "I'm 54, where do y'all get 29 from? I was born in 1965."

He added: "That's why you shouldn't believe everything you see on the internet."

What does he do?

It's not known if Sir Cruse does anything outside of his YouTube career.

He regularly uploads new videos to his account - usually at least twice a week - which shows he's making YouTube his main source of work.

His videos are sometimes over four hours long and he's best known for discussing hair technique.

360 waves is the hairstyle that's associated with him as most of his videos are about how to get the style.

Sir Cruse sometimes posts reaction videos to others who've achieved or attempted the style.

On TikTok he has 374.3 thousand followers and his Instagram figures are at a similar place.

He often uses his TikTok account to comment on other viral videos, from wave hairstyle videos to other unrelated topics.

What's his net worth?

The same website that claimed Sir Cruse was 29 years old also says his net worth is $100,000.

However, this hasn't been confirmed and might well be incorrect too.

He aim is to reach 100,000 YouTube subscribers - if achieved his worth is likely to increase significantly.
