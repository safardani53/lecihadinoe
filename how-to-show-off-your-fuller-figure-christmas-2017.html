We reveal how to wear Christmas party outfits that show off your fuller figure on the dance floor

PARTY season is upon us – so make sure you stand out on the dance floor.

The high street is awash with sequins and statement pieces that are both fun AND flattering.

And it’s not all fancy frocks, there are also power suits and slinky separates to rock around the tree in this year.

Here, SIOBHAN O’CONNOR styles the best pieces that work to show off your curves.

Green dress

TIP: This frock ticks all the boxes – ruching flatters the stomach, while sleeves are perfect for those who aren’t confident about their arms.

We love the rich green hue too.

Navy jumpsuit

TIP: Satin fabrics can be tricky – but a loose-fitting style will skim over your silhouette.

Accessorise with a fun faux fur coat to detract attention from areas you’re not confident.

MOST READ IN FABULOUS

Pink blouse

TIP: The knot detail on the top will cinch in the waist and disguise any problem areas.

These glitter trousers will skim over your bum and hips and widen towards the ankle, giving a flattering shape.

Red power suit

TIP: Sharpen up your party look with a power suit. Go for fitted jackets to emphasise your curves and slip a sequin cami on underneath for a glam touch.

Velvet asymmetric dress

TIP: Velvet and asymmetric – this great dress ticks two trends in one.

The figure-hugging fit will sculpt your curves while the sleeves will let you bare one arm without feeling too exposed.
