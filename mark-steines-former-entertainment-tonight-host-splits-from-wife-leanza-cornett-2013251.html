Mark Steines, Former Entertainment Tonight Host, Splits From Wife Leanza Cornett

Sad news from Hallmark Channel's Home & Family host Mark Steines. The television journalist — who previously appeared on Entertainment Tonight — is separating from his wife of 17 years, actress Leanza Cornett, the couple told Us Weekly exclusively. They have two children together, Kai Harper, 10, and Avery James, 9.

The spouses confirmed the split in a joint statement: "After a great deal of thought and consideration, we have decided to dissolve our 17-year marriage. At this time our concern is for our boys as we help them with this difficult transition. We ask that you respect our privacy during this difficult time."
