Rotherham vs Middlesbrough live stream, TV channel, team news, and kick off time

MIDDLESBROUGH face a trip to Rotherham as they aim to leapfrog Derby into the Championship play-offs.

The Rams' 1-1 draw at Swansea means that Tony Pulis' Boro will extend their season if they win in South Yorkshire and Derby fail to beat West Brom at home.

Hosts Rotherham were relegated last week after they lost 2-1 at West Brom while Millwall got the point they needed to survive at home to Stoke.

When is Rotherham vs Middlesbrough?

Rotherham take on Middlesbrough on Sunday, May 5.

As all final day games, the match will kick off at 12.30pm.

It will be played at the New York Stadium.

Is Rotherham vs Middlesbrough on TV and can I live stream it?

Unfortunately, Rotherham vs Middlesbrough will not be on TV as Sky Sports have announced that Derby vs West Brom will be their televised game on the Championship final day.

But you will be able to follow all the action on the SunSport LIVE BLOG.
