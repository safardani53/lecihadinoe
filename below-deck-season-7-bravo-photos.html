Below Deck Season 7 New Cast: Photos Hollywood Life

Bravo’s ‘Below Deck’ debuted on July 1, 2013 and has since gone on to create spin-off series and new high seas adventures, with plenty of drama. The show profiles a group of young people all working on the same yacht that is merely 100 feet long. The people who work aboard, known as Yachties, live on the vessel while ensuring that their clients’ every needs are met. The whole mentality for the crew is to ‘work hard, and play hard’ with tons of drama from the 85 season the series has had so far. Now, the show is going into its seventh season, with Captain Lee Rosbach and Kate Chastain returning with a number of new faces to cater to the various needs of their clients as they travel to Thailand. It’s bound to be a hot and dramatic season in one of the most beautiful places on land and sea.
