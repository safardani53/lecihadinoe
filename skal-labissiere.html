Who is Skal Labissiere:

Skal Labissiere is a famous Basketball Player. He was born on March 18, 1996 and his birthplace is Port-au-Prince, Haiti. Skal is also well known as, 7-foot power forward/center who became one of the top recruits in the 2015-16 freshman class, committing to the University of Kentucky. After one year at Kentucky, he was selected 28th overall in the 2016 NBA Draft, joining the Sacramento Kings.

Skal is originated from Haiti. He was recruited to play for Kentucky by head coach John Calipari.
