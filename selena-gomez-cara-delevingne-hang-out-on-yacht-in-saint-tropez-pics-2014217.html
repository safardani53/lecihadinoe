Selena Gomez, Cara Delevingne Hang Out on Yacht in Saint-Tropez: Pics

Happy early birthday! Selena Gomez soaked up the sun in Saint-Tropez on a yacht with supermodel and new friend Cara Delevingne on Monday, July 21, a day before her 22nd birthday.

The “Come & Get It” songstress, who showed skin in revealing ensembles at the Ischia Global Film and Music Festival in Ischia, Italy, over the weekend, indulged in a little R&R, donning an all white one-piece swimsuit.

Justin Bieber‘s on-and-off girlfriend was joined by Delevingne, who wore a black bikini top and red shorts. The two friends enjoyed one another’s company, as well as friend Eli Mizrahi, as they splashed around in the water. Prior to boarding the yacht, the girls shared a picture of themselves after landing in a helicopter.
